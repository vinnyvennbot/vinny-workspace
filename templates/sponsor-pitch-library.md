## CUSTOMIZATION CHECKLIST (Use Before Every Send)

**Pre-send verification (MANDATORY):**
- [ ] Contact name researched and confirmed (LinkedIn/website)
- [ ] Email address verified (not generic info@)
- [ ] Event date verified with `date -j -f "%Y-%m-%d" "YYYY-MM-DD" "+%A %B %d, %Y"`
- [ ] Venue confirmed from event README
- [ ] Capacity numbers accurate
- [ ] Brand-specific research complete (recent campaigns, values, products)
- [ ] Partnership amount appropriate for brand tier ($3K-$5K brands vs $50K corporate)
- [ ] No internal IDs (EVT-001, etc.) in email body
- [ ] Body uses single quotes if dollar amounts mentioned
- [ ] Professional signature included

**Brand research sources:**
- Company website "About" and "Partnerships" pages
- Instagram recent posts (brand voice, visual style)
- LinkedIn company page (recent initiatives)
- Press releases (new products, campaigns)

**Personalization minimum standard:**
- At least 2 brand-specific details (product names, brand values, recent campaigns)
- Clear connection between brand and event theme (not generic)
- Specific deliverables tied to brand goals (not copy-paste)
