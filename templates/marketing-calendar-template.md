## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram (@vinny.vennsf)
**Posting Frequency:**
- Feed posts: 2-3 per week during campaign
- Stories: Daily during campaign, multiple per day final week
- Reels: 1-2 per event cycle
- Carousels: 1-2 per event cycle

**Best Posting Times:**
- Feed posts: 10 AM - 12 PM, 5 PM - 7 PM PST
- Stories: Throughout day, peak 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM
- Reels: 11 AM or 6 PM (algorithm favors these times)

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 15-20 hashtags per post
- Mix: Brand (3-5) + Event-specific (5-7) + Discovery (7-10)
- Place in first comment, not caption

### Email
**List Segments:**
1. **Past attendees** (highest priority, highest conversion)
2. **Engaged non-attendees** (opened/clicked past emails)
3. **Cold subscribers** (haven't engaged in 3+ months)

**Email Frequency:**
- Pre-launch: 1 email
- Launch week: 2 emails (announcement, early bird reminder)
- Mid-campaign: 1-2 emails (social proof, urgency)
- Event week: 2-3 emails (details, reminder, day-of)
- Post-event: 1-2 emails (thanks, photos)

**Subject Line Formula:**
- Announcement: "[Emoji] [Event Name] - [Unique Value Prop]"
- Reminder: "Last chance: [Benefit] at [Event Name]"
- Urgency: "[X] days until [Event Name] | Tickets going fast"

### Luma / Eventbrite
**Page Optimization:**
- Hero image: High-quality, event theme, people enjoying themselves
- Description: Hook (1 sentence) → Details (bullets) → CTA
- FAQ section: Address top 5-10 questions proactively
- Ticket tiers: Early bird, General, VIP (if applicable)
- Refund policy: Clear, reassuring

## Emergency Protocols

### If Event Underselling (< 30% capacity at Week -2):
1. **Immediate:** Flash sale (24-48 hour discount)
2. **Content:** Increase posting frequency to daily
3. **Partners:** Request boost/cross-promotion
4. **Email:** Send targeted email to warm leads
5. **Outreach:** Personal invites to past high-engagers

### If Event Overselling (sold out early):
1. **Immediate:** Announce sold out status (creates FOMO)
2. **Waitlist:** Create and promote waitlist
3. **Next event:** Tease next event immediately
4. **Capacity:** Check if venue can accommodate more
5. **Premium tier:** Consider adding VIP tier at higher price

### If Last-Minute Changes (venue, date, etc.):
1. **Immediate:** Email all ticket holders
2. **Instagram:** Post announcement to feed + stories
3. **Refund offer:** Provide easy refund option
4. **Apology:** Personal, genuine apology from team
5. **Bonus:** Offer something extra (free drink, upgrade, discount on next event)
