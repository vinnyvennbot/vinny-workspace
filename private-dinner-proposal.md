# Venn Event Proposal: Intimate Private Dinner

## Event Concept
**"The Gathering: An Intimate Evening"**  
Exclusive 30-person private dining experience that embodies Venn's ethos of unique, story-driven connections in an intimate, upscale setting.

## Target Specifications  
- **Capacity:** 30 people exactly
- **Budget Target:** <$70/person total cost  
- **Venue Vibe:** Warm, intimate, upscale atmosphere
- **Target Ticket Price:** $45-65 (depending on final value proposition)

## Revenue Model Analysis
### Base Scenarios:
- **Conservative:** $45/ticket × 30 = $1,350 revenue
- **Premium:** $65/ticket × 30 = $1,950 revenue
- **Cost Target:** $2,100 max total cost (<$70/person)
- **Profit Range:** $0-$600 (break-even to 44% margin)

### Value Drivers for Premium Pricing:
- Multi-course curated menu by renowned chef
- Wine pairings or premium cocktails  
- Unique venue with story/history
- Exclusive access (private room/space)
- Special programming (wine sommelier, chef interaction)
