## Risk Mitigation

### Weather Risk (CRITICAL for outdoor event)
**Strategies:**
1. **Backup date:** Built into contracts (reschedule within 30 days)
2. **Tent rental:** Add $2,000 for partial coverage (recommendation)
3. **Refund policy:** 80% refund if weather cancellation (insurance covers)
4. **Season selection:** April-June or August-September (lowest rain probability)

**Recommended:** Add $2,000 tent rental to fixed costs for peace of mind

### Capacity Risk
**Strategies:**
1. **Early bird pricing:** $75 for first 50 tickets (guaranteed base)
2. **Group sales:** 10+ groups get $5 discount (fills capacity faster)
3. **Influencer comps:** 10-15 comped tickets = 1,000+ social reach

### Sponsor Risk
**Strategies:**
1. **Start outreach 10 weeks out:** Longer lead time for larger sponsors
2. **Tiered packages:** $2K-5K options (flexible entry points)
3. **Backup list:** 10+ potential sponsors identified before launch
