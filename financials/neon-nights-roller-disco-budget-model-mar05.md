## Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Factors

**1. Liability/Insurance**
- Skating = inherent injury risk
- Alcohol + skating = elevated risk
- Insurance: $1,200+ non-negotiable
- **Mitigation:** Strict waiver system, on-site EMT, wristband alcohol control

**2. Venue Availability**
- Church of 8 Wheels = legendary but busy
- May have limited private event slots
- **Mitigation:** Book 12+ weeks out, have Warehouse backup plan

**3. Skating Skill Barrier**
- Some guests may be intimidated/scared
- Could impact ticket sales if market research shows low skating confidence
- **Mitigation:** Emphasize "all skill levels," provide skate angels, beginner periods

**4. Production Complexity**
- Significant lighting/sound needs
- Décor setup time (4-6 hours)
- **Mitigation:** Hire experienced production company, extended load-in time

### Mitigation Strategies

**Safety First:**
- ✅ Comprehensive waiver system (digital + paper)
- ✅ On-site EMT (non-negotiable)
- ✅ Skate instructors roaming
- ✅ "Slow skate" periods for beginners
- ✅ Alcohol service cut-off 1 hour before end

**Marketing De-Risk:**
- ✅ Pre-event skate lesson partnership (discount for ticket holders)
- ✅ Emphasize "beginner-friendly" in all marketing
- ✅ Show video of people having fun regardless of skill
