## Strategic Recommendations

### ✅ PURSUE: Scenario B with Optimizations
1. **Lock DecoDance Bar** - built-in Art Deco aesthetic reduces decor costs
2. **Book Radio Gatsby** (6-piece) - authentic 1920s jazz, proven SF reputation
3. **Target $8K total sponsors:**
   - **Hendrick's Gin** ($4K) - natural fit for Bee's Knees signature cocktail
   - **Fashion boutique** ($2K) - Best Dressed contest partnership
   - **Wine brand or champagne** ($2K) - French 75 cocktail tie-in
4. **Optimize F&B:**
   - Negotiate bar package: $30/person (2.5 drinks average, house spirits)
   - Passed apps: $25/person (elegant but not excessive - 5-6 pieces per guest)
   - Target $55/person total F&B (vs. current $60)
5. **Ticket Strategy:**
   - Early bird: $75 (first 40 tickets) - drives early momentum
   - Regular: $85 (next 40 tickets)
   - Last call: $95 (final 20 tickets) - scarcity urgency
   - Average: ~$85/person

**With these optimizations:**
- Revenue: $8,500 (tickets) + $8,000 (sponsor) = $16,500
- Expenses: $10,900 (fixed) + $5,500 (variable @ $55/person) = $16,400
- **Net: +$100 (break-even)** ✅

### ⚠️ DEFER: Scenario A (Premium)
- Requires $140+ ticket price for break-even - too high for SF market
- Risk: Unsold inventory, low attendance, brand damage
- **Only pursue if:** Pre-sold 80+ tickets at $120+ via exclusive partnerships

### ❌ AVOID: Scenario C (Budget)
- Compromises core concept (smaller band, minimal production)
- Risk: Event feels "cheap" despite 1920s branding
- Minimal margin for error - any ticket shortfall = loss

## Risk Mitigation

### Financial Risks
1. **Ticket sales shortfall:** 
   - **Mitigation:** Early bird pricing creates urgency, social media ads target 1920s enthusiasts
   - **Backup:** Reduce capacity to 80 guests, scale production costs proportionally
   
2. **Sponsor acquisition failure:**
   - **Mitigation:** Start outreach 8 weeks pre-event, multiple brand targets per category
   - **Backup:** Increase ticket price to $95-100 if sponsors fall through

3. **F&B cost overruns:**
   - **Mitigation:** Fixed-price catering contract, confirmed quote in writing
   - **Backup:** Reduce cocktail hours from 3.5 to 3 hours, scale appetizers

### Operational Risks
1. **Band cancellation:**
   - **Mitigation:** Contract backup band (Harrison Jazz Band), deposit/insurance
   
2. **Venue availability:**
   - **Mitigation:** Have 2-3 venue options negotiated simultaneously, flexible dates

3. **Low attendance:**
   - **Mitigation:** Minimum viable: 80 guests (break-even with sponsor). Cancel if <60 sold by 2 weeks out.
