## Risk Mitigation

### Financial Risks
1. **Sponsor acquisition failure:**
   - **Mitigation:** Start outreach 10 weeks early, target 15+ tech companies simultaneously
   - **Backup:** Increase ticket to $110 average if only $15K secured (vs. $22K target)

2. **Ticket sales shortfall:**
   - **Mitigation:** Strong presale push, early bird pricing, astronomy community partnerships
   - **Backup:** Reduce capacity to 200 guests, scale variable costs (-$2,900), break-even at lower attendance

3. **Venue availability:**
   - **Mitigation:** Book 9-12 months in advance (planetariums book fast)
   - **Backup:** Have CAS and Lawrence Hall as alternate options

### Operational Risks
1. **Transportation logistics:**
   - **Risk:** Guests struggle to reach Oakland hilltop location
   - **Mitigation:** Shuttle from BART every 20 min, rideshare promo codes, clear directions
   - **Backup:** Partner with Uber/Lyft for subsidized rides ($10 credit per guest)

2. **Planetarium technical issues:**
   - **Risk:** Projection system fails, show can't run
   - **Mitigation:** Venue has backup systems, test run 48 hours before event
   - **Backup:** DJ-only format with VJ visuals if planetarium fails (announce transparently, offer partial refunds)

3. **Alcohol restrictions:**
   - **Risk:** Science venues may limit alcohol service
   - **Mitigation:** Confirm policies in writing during venue contract
   - **Backup:** "Cosmic mocktails" featured prominently if alcohol limited
