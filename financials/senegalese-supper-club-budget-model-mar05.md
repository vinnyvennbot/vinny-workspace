## Risk Mitigation

### Cultural Sensitivity Risks
1. **Risk:** Cultural appropriation concerns
   - **Mitigation:** Center Senegalese chef + cultural advisor in all decisions
   - **Backup:** Full transparency about collaboration, profit-sharing with cultural org

2. **Risk:** Authenticity questioned by Senegalese community
   - **Mitigation:** Partner with established Senegalese restaurant/chef (Teranga, Bissap Baobab)
   - **Backup:** Invite Senegalese community leaders as guests, incorporate feedback

### Financial Risks
3. **Risk:** Low ticket sales (unfamiliar cuisine)
   - **Mitigation:** Educational pre-marketing ("What is Senegalese food?"), early bird pricing
   - **Backup:** Reduce capacity to 40 guests, scale costs proportionally

4. **Risk:** Chef/restaurant partnership falls through
   - **Mitigation:** Have 2 backup chef options, residential venue as fallback
   - **Backup:** Pivot to Nigerian or Ethiopian cuisine (still West African focus)
