## Risk Mitigation

### Weather Risk (Lower than indoor events)
**Strategies:**
1. **Season selection:** April-October (lower rain risk)
2. **Backup plan:** Venue indoor/covered area if available
3. **Weather insurance:** $200-300 (optional)
4. **Flexible date:** Built into contracts (reschedule within 30 days)

### Co-Host Risk
**If tech co-host doesn't materialize:**
1. **Plan B:** Increase ticket price to $85 GA / $120 VIP
2. **Plan C:** Reduce capacity to 80 guests (tighter cost control)
3. **Plan D:** Find lifestyle brand co-host (Lululemon, Outdoor Voices)

### VIP Take Rate Risk
**If < 20% VIP conversion:**
1. Early bird VIP pricing ($95 vs $110)
2. Enhanced VIP benefits (professional photo session)
3. Group VIP packages (4-pack discount)
