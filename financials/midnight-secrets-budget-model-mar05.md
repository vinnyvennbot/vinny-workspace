## Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Factors

**1. Niche Appeal**
- Speakeasy theme = smaller target audience vs. garden party/art gallery
- Date night focus = couples only (limits solo ticket sales)
- Jazz music = specific taste (not universal appeal)

**2. Venue Dependency**
- Authentic speakeasy venues are expensive
- Limited negotiating power (established bars)
- Revenue share may not be possible

**3. Pricing Ceiling**
- $135-150 is high for date night event
- May struggle to sell out at premium pricing
- Competing with Michelin-star dinners at same price point

**4. Small Capacity**
- 60-70 guests = limited revenue upside
- Fixed costs don't scale down proportionally
- One bad weather night = major loss

### Mitigations

**If proceeding:**
1. ✅ Launch ticket sales 6 weeks early (gauge demand)
2. ✅ Early bird pricing ($115) to lock in base attendance
3. ✅ Partner with jazz venues for cross-promotion
4. ✅ Emphasize exclusivity in marketing (limited capacity)
5. ✅ Have backup date if sell-through is weak
