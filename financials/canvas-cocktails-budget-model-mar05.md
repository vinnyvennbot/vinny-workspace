## Risk Mitigation

**Financial Risks:**
1. **Low ticket sales** - Mitigate: Early bird pricing, influencer partnerships
2. **Sponsor shortfall** - Mitigate: Start outreach 8 weeks out, have backup list
3. **Low art sales** - Mitigate: Price points $200-800 (accessible), payment plans
4. **Food/bev overrun** - Mitigate: Fixed per-person contract, confirmed headcount 5 days prior

**Breakage Opportunities:**
- 10-15% no-shows = food/bev savings (~$600)
- Last-minute ticket sales (walk-ups if promoted)
- Raffle/auction add-on revenue
