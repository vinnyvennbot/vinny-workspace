# Venn Event Proposal: Western Line Dancing Experience

## Event Concept
**"Wild West Nights: Line Dancing & Libations"**  
An authentic western line dancing experience that brings together great music, dancing, drinks, and community in a rustic, welcoming atmosphere. Think honky-tonk meets San Francisco sophistication.

## Target Specifications
- **Capacity:** 60-100 people (scalable based on venue)
- **Budget Range:** $40-70/person depending on programming
- **Vibe:** Authentic western, fun, social, accessible to beginners
- **Revenue Target:** $2,400-7,000 gross revenue

## Revenue Model Analysis

### Ticket Pricing Scenarios:
1. **Basic Package ($40):** Venue, instruction, DJ, light snacks
   - 60 people = $2,400 revenue
   - 100 people = $4,000 revenue

2. **Premium Package ($55):** + Open bar, BBQ dinner, live band
   - 60 people = $3,300 revenue  
   - 100 people = $5,500 revenue

3. **VIP Package ($70):** + Premium spirits, catered dinner, special perks
   - 60 people = $4,200 revenue
   - 100 people = $7,000 revenue

## Programming & Experience Design

### Core Programming Elements:
1. **Line Dancing Instruction** (1-1.5 hours)
   - Professional instructor for beginners
   - Popular line dances: Electric Slide, Cupid Shuffle, Cotton-Eyed Joe
   - Progressive difficulty throughout night

2. **Music & Entertainment**
   - Option A: Professional DJ with country/western playlist
   - Option B: Live country band (higher cost, higher value)
   - Mix of classic country, modern country, and line dancing favorites

3. **Food & Beverage**
   - **Basic:** Light snacks, cash bar
   - **Premium:** BBQ buffet, open bar with whiskey specialties
   - **VIP:** Full dinner service, premium liquor, specialty cocktails

### Special Programming Ideas:
- **Line Dancing Competition** with prizes
- **Western Costume Contest** (optional dress-up)
- **"Learn a New Dance" workshops** throughout the evening
- **Photo booth** with western props
- **Mechanical bull** (if venue permits)

## Financial Model (100-person event)

### Conservative Scenario ($40 ticket):
- **Revenue:** $4,000
- **Costs:**
  - Venue: $500-800
  - Instructor: $300
  - DJ: $400  
  - Light snacks: $500
  - Cash bar (venue handles)
  - **Total Cost:** ~$1,700
  - **Profit:** $2,300 (57% margin)

### Premium Scenario ($55 ticket):  
- **Revenue:** $5,500
- **Costs:**
  - Venue: $500-800
  - Instructor: $400
  - Live Band: $1,200
  - BBQ Catering: $1,800
  - Open Bar: $1,500
  - **Total Cost:** ~$5,200
