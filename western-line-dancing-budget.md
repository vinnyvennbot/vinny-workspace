# Western Line Dancing Event - Budget & Profit Forecast

## Event Overview: "Wild West Nights"
**Target Capacity:** 75 people | **Target Ticket Price:** $45-65

---

## SCENARIO 1: BASIC PACKAGE ($45 ticket, 75 attendees)

### **REVENUE**
- Ticket Sales: $45 × 75 = **$3,375**

### **EXPENSES**

#### **VENUE: Presidio Log Cabin**
- Venue Rental: $1,000
- Minimum Catering Requirement: $15/person × 75 = $1,125
- **Venue Total: $2,125 ($28.33/person)**

#### **VENDORS**
- Line Dancing Instructor: $250
- DJ (4 hours): $500  
- Basic Sound System: $150
- **Vendor Total: $900 ($12/person)**

#### **FOOD & BEVERAGE**
- Light Western Snacks: $10/person × 75 = $750
- Cash Bar (venue handles, no cost to us)
- **F&B Total: $750 ($10/person)**

#### **MARKETING & OPERATIONS**
- Photography: $300
- Marketing Materials: $100
- Platform Fees (Luma): $100
- **Marketing Total: $500 ($6.67/person)**

### **TOTAL EXPENSES: $4,275 ($57/person)**
### **NET PROFIT: -$900 (LOSS - over budget)**

---

## SCENARIO 3: PROFITABLE PACKAGE ($65 ticket, 100 attendees)

### **REVENUE**
- Ticket Sales: $65 × 100 = **$6,500**
- Volo Sponsorship: $1,500
- Whiskey Brand Sponsorship: $1,000 (provides alcohol)
- **Total Revenue: $9,000**

### **EXPENSES**

#### **VENUE: The Chapel**
- Venue Rental: $2,500
- Sound System Included: $0
- **Venue Total: $2,500 ($25/person)**

#### **VENDORS**
- Line Dancing Instructor: $400
- Live Country Band (3-piece): $1,500
- **Vendor Total: $1,900 ($19/person)**

#### **FOOD & BEVERAGE**
- BBQ Catering: $22/person × 100 = $2,200
- Sponsored Open Bar: $0 (covered by whiskey sponsor)
- **F&B Total: $2,200 ($22/person)**

#### **MARKETING & OPERATIONS**
- Photography/Video: $500
- Influencer Partnerships: $600
- Platform, Materials, Staff: $300
- **Marketing Total: $1,400 ($14/person)**

### **TOTAL EXPENSES: $8,000 ($80/person)**
### **NET PROFIT: $1,000 (11% margin)**

---

## RECOMMENDATION: SCENARIO 3
**Why:** Only profitable scenario that maintains quality experience while staying within reasonable per-person costs through sponsorships and higher capacity.

**Critical Success Factors:**
1. Secure Volo + Whiskey Brand sponsorships
2. Achieve 100-person capacity
3. Premium venue with built-in capabilities reduces vendor costs

---

**Cost Estimate Sources:**
- Venue costs: Industry standard SF event space pricing
- Vendor costs: Market research on SF DJs/bands/instructors  
- Catering: SF BBQ catering company estimates
- Sponsorship: Conservative estimates based on brand partnership potential
