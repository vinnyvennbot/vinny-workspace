### WHAT IS THIS?

Western Line Dancing Night is San Francisco's answer to the question: "Where can I actually DANCE and have fun without feeling awkward?"

This isn't a country bar. This isn't a tourist trap. This is a fully immersive night of connection, movement, and unforgettable energy—with a Western twist.

Whether you've never two-stepped in your life or you secretly know every Luke Bryan song, you belong here.

### WHAT TO EXPECT

**🤠 PROFESSIONAL LINE DANCING LESSONS**  
Never danced before? Perfect. Our instructors will teach you every step, starting with the absolute basics. By the end of the night, you'll be moving like you've been doing this for years.

**🎵 LIVE COUNTRY DJ**  
From classic Willie Nelson to modern Kane Brown, our DJ knows how to keep the energy high and the dance floor packed. Expect singalongs, boot-stomping beats, and maybe even a little "Cotton Eye Joe."

**🎯 MECHANICAL BULL CHALLENGE**  
Think you can last 8 seconds? Prove it. Compete for bragging rights (and Instagram glory) on our professional-grade mechanical bull. Soft landing guaranteed.

**🍖 BBQ & CRAFT COCKTAILS**  
Fuel up with authentic BBQ platters, craft cocktails with a Western twist, and cold beers. Dancing burns calories—we'll help you earn them back.

**✨ THE VIBE**  
This is what nightlife should feel like: 200 people dancing together, laughing at themselves, meeting strangers who become friends by the end of the night. Zero pretense. Maximum fun.

Absolutely not! Our professional instructors will teach everyone from complete beginners to experienced dancers. If you can walk, you can line dance.

**What if I come alone?**  
Perfect! This event is designed for connection. You'll be dancing with 200+ people, and our instructors create a welcoming environment where everyone mingles.

**Is there an age limit?**  
Yes, this is a 21+ event. Valid ID required at the door.

**What should I wear?**  
Comfortable closed-toe shoes (boots encouraged but not required) and clothes you can move in. Think jeans, plaid, denim—Western vibes welcome but not mandatory.

**Is food and drink included?**

**Can I ride the mechanical bull multiple times?**  
Yes! The bull will be open all night (capacity permitting). Try to beat your high score.
