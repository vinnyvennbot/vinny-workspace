## EVENT REQUIREMENTS

**For Canvas & Cocktails to work:**
- 80-120 guest capacity
- Tables/easels for painting setup
- Bar area for cocktail service
- Good lighting (art instruction needs visibility)
- Easy load-in (art supplies, canvases, portable bar)
- Ideally: Natural light, Instagram-worthy aesthetic

**Budget Target:**
- Venue: $2,000-3,500 (4-hour rental)
- Art supplies: $1,200 (canvases, paint, brushes for 100 guests)
- Instruction: $500-800 (2 art instructors)
- Cocktails/catering: $3,500-4,500 ($35-45/person for drinks + light bites)
- Total event cost: $7,200-10,300
- Ticket price: $75-85/person
- Break-even: 95-120 guests (85% capacity)

**Sponsor Need:** $6-8K sponsor makes this comfortably profitable
