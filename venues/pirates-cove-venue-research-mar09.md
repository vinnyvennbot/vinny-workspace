## 🏆 TOP RECOMMENDATION: SS JEREMIAH O'BRIEN

**SS Jeremiah O'Brien**  
Pier 35 North, 1454 The Embarcadero  
San Francisco, CA 94133 (Fisherman's Wharf)

### Venue Specifications
- **Type:** Authentic WWII Liberty Ship (last unaltered Liberty ship)
- **Status:** National Historic Landmark, fully operational museum ship
- **Built:** June 19, 1943 (Maine) - launched 56 days after construction start
- **History:** 1976 Paris Wine Tasting participant, D-Day veteran, transatlantic convoy service
- **Engines:** Original steam engines still operational (used in filming Titanic movie)

### Capacity
- **Cargo Hold (Primary Space):**
  - Seated dinner with dance floor: 170 guests
  - Standing reception: 250 guests
- **Officer's Salon (Intimate Option):**
  - Up to 16 people for small meetings or intimate dinners

### Contact Information
- **Mailing Address:** National Liberty Ship Memorial, Inc., Pier 45, Suite 4A, San Francisco, CA 94133
- **Museum Location:** Pier 35 North, 1454 The Embarcadero
- **Contact Method:** Contact form on website (no direct email/phone published)
- **Website:** ssjeremiahobrien.org
- **Admission:** $12 adults, $6 children (general museum admission reference)

### Unique Features for Pirate Event
🚢 **Authentic Maritime Experience** - Real WWII ship = ultimate pirate authenticity  
⚓ **Multiple Decks** - Guests can explore engine room, gun deck, bridge, crew quarters  
🌊 **San Francisco Bay Backdrop** - Waterfront views, Embarcadero location  
🎬 **Hollywood Connection** - Steam engines used in Titanic film (conversation piece)  
📚 **WWII History** - D-Day participation, convoy service stories = storytelling potential  
🏴‍☠️ **Cargo Hold Atmosphere** - Perfect for "treasure hold" or ship's mess pirate feast  

### Event Fit Analysis

**PERFECT MATCHES:**
- ✅ **Real ship = unbeatable authenticity** for pirate theme
- ✅ **Multiple levels/rooms** = exploration/adventure experience
- ✅ **Industrial cargo hold** = rough, authentic maritime vibe (not polished ballroom)
- ✅ **Fisherman's Wharf location** = easy access, tourist-friendly area
- ✅ **Bay cruises available** = potential add-on for premium experience

**STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES:**
- 🎯 **Storytelling gold**: "Board a real WWII ship that survived the Atlantic"
- 🎯 **Photo opportunities**: Authentic ship deck, engine room, gun emplacements
- 🎯 **Multiple experience zones**: Cargo hold party, deck views, engine room tours
- 🎯 **Weather flexibility**: Indoor cargo hold + outdoor deck options

**CONSIDERATIONS:**

- ⚠️ Catering logistics: Need to bring equipment onboard
