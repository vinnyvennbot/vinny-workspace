## 🎯 THE STRATEGY

LinkedIn doesn't have an "age" filter, so we target by **graduation year**:
- **21-25 years old in 2026** = graduated **2020-2024** (undergrad) or **2022-2026** (if masters)
- Location: San Francisco Bay Area
- Use scraping tools to export 1,000s of profiles

---

## 📋 STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

### **Step 1: Build LinkedIn Search URLs**

LinkedIn People Search with filters:

**Recent College Grads (2022-2024):**
```
https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/people/?geoUrn=%5B%22102277331%22%5D&schoolFilter=%5B%22recent%22%5D&origin=FACETED_SEARCH
```

**Specific Graduation Years:**
- 2024 grads: `&graduationYear=2024`
- 2023 grads: `&graduationYear=2023`
- 2022 grads: `&graduationYear=2022`
- 2021 grads: `&graduationYear=2021`
- 2020 grads: `&graduationYear=2020`

**Location Filter:**
- San Francisco Bay Area: `&geoUrn=%5B%22102277331%22%5D`

### **Step 2: Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Recommended)**

**Cost:** $99.99/month (cancel after 1 month)

**Filters to use:**
1. Location: San Francisco Bay Area
2. School: (leave open for all schools)
3. Years at current company: 0-2 years
4. Graduation date: 2020-2024

**Search URL format:**
```
https://www.linkedin.com/sales/search/people?geoIncluded=102277331&graduationYearMax=2024&graduationYearMin=2020
```

This will show you 10,000+ results.

## 🤖 SCRAPING TOOLS (Export to CSV)

### **Option 1: Phantombuster** (Recommended)
- **Cost:** $69/month
- **Tool:** LinkedIn Search Export
- **Process:**
  1. Sign up at phantombuster.com
  2. Use "LinkedIn Search Export" automation
  3. Paste your LinkedIn search URL
  4. Connect LinkedIn account
  5. Export to CSV (name, profile URL, headline, location)
- **Limit:** 2,500 profiles/month on basic plan

### **Option 2: Evaboot** (Sales Navigator only)
- **Cost:** $39/month
- **Tool:** Evaboot Chrome Extension
- **Process:**
  1. Install Evaboot extension
  2. Run Sales Navigator search
  3. Click "Export with Evaboot"
  4. Get CSV with emails (if available)
- **Limit:** 2,500 leads/month

### **Option 3: Bardeen.ai** (Free tier available)
- **Cost:** Free for 100 credits, then $10/month
- **Tool:** LinkedIn to Google Sheets scraper
- **Process:**
  1. Install Bardeen extension
  2. Run LinkedIn search
  3. Click Bardeen icon → "Save LinkedIn search to Google Sheets"
- **Limit:** 100 profiles/month free
