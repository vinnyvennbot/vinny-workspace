# The Artist's Loft - Live Art Battle & Paint Party
**Research Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Concept Status:** Ideation (Research Only - No Vendor Contact)  
**Target Capacity:** 80-120 guests  
**Venn Brand Alignment:** ★★★★☆ (Creative social experience, spectator sport + participation)

## Concept Overview
Transform guests from spectators to creators. First half: watch professional artists compete in a live "art battle" (30-min timed paintings with live judging). Second half: guided group paint & sip where guests create their own pieces inspired by the battle. Wine/cocktails throughout, DJ spinning, final gallery walk where guests vote on their favorite battle piece. Combines spectacle with hands-on creativity.
