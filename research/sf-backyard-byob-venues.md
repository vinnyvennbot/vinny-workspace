# SF Backyard Venues with BYOB/Flexible Alcohol Policies

**Research Date:** 2026-02-21  
**Request:** Backyards available for rent in SF where we can bring our own alcohol (like Neon co-working)

## 🎯 TOP RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. **The Academy SF** 
- **Location:** Castro District
- **Outdoor Space:** Gorgeous heated outdoor garden terrace with deck, fire pits, designer outdoor furniture
- **Capacity:** Multiple spaces (full venue can accommodate larger groups)
- **Features:**
  - Top floor club lounge with wine bar
  - Basement speakeasy with full bar
  - Main floor with blackjack table
  - **Heated outdoor garden terrace** (key feature)
- **Use Cases:** Receptions, client events, fundraisers, cocktail parties, off-sites
- **Alcohol:** Membership-based club - likely flexible for private events
- **Website:** https://www.academy-sf.com
- **Status:** Active event space with regular programming
- **PartySlate:** https://www.partyslate.com/venues/the-academy-sf

### 2. **The Magic Patio**
- **Location:** San Francisco (exact address TBD via contact)
- **Outdoor Space:** Backyard patio venue - "one of the most unique, magical places in SF"
- **Capacity:** Up to 50 standing, 40 dining
- **Pricing:** $3,500 minimum spend (weekdays), $5,000 (weekends)
- **Features:**
  - Black walls, brick, real wood floors
  - Bar inside
  - Elegant antique atmosphere
- **Alcohol:** Venue provides alcohol (license until 12 AM) - **NO BYO allowed**
- **External Catering:** Allowed
- **Music:** Play your own, bring DJ, no noise restrictions
- **Promoted/Ticketed Events:** Allowed
- **Contact:** Andrew Evans (Tagvenue Partner)
- **Tagvenue:** https://www.tagvenue.com/us/rooms/san-francisco/34735/the-magic-patio/entire-space
- **Note:** ⚠️ NO BYOB - but venue provides alcohol

### 3. **Presidio Lawn Rentals**
- **Location:** The Presidio, San Francisco
- **Outdoor Space:** Multiple lawn areas available for rental
- **Alcohol:** **YES, alcohol is allowed** at events
- **Use Cases:** Outdoor weddings, corporate events, gatherings
- **Permit Required:** Special Use Permits required
- **Website:** https://presidio.gov/plan-an-event/lawn-rentals-and-special-use-permits
- **Note:** Public outdoor space - permits required, but confirmed alcohol allowed

### 4. **City View Commissary**
- **Location:** Treasure Island, San Francisco
- **Outdoor Space:** Private patio
- **Pricing:** Includes 4 hours + prepaid menu options
- **Features:**
  - City views
  - Full bar available
  - Private patio space
- **Use Cases:** Corporate meetings, art festivals, baptisms, black tie events, bridal showers
- **Peerspace:** Listed on Peerspace
- **Note:** Has bar service - BYOB policy unclear, needs verification
