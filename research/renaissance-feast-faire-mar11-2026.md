# Renaissance Feast & Faire - Medieval Banquet Experience
**Event ID:** evt-renaissance-feast-mar11
**Generated:** March 11, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Target Capacity:** 100-150 guests
**Theme:** Immersive medieval banquet with period entertainment, feasting, and interactive roleplay

## Concept Overview
Transport guests to a Renaissance-era feast complete with long communal tables, pewter goblets, roasted meats, mead flowing freely, and wandering minstrels. This isn't just dinner—it's an immersive theatrical experience where guests are encouraged to dress in period costume, participate in toasts and revelry, and enjoy authentic medieval entertainment. Think "Game of Thrones meets Bridgerton" with a focus on communal celebration and spectacle.

**Key Experience Elements:**
- Grand entrance with herald announcement for each guest
- Long banquet tables with communal seating (break down social barriers)
- Multi-course medieval feast served family-style
- Mead, ale, and spiced wine flowing throughout
- Live entertainment: jesters, sword fighters, acrobats, musicians
- Interactive elements: archery station, coat of arms creation, fortune teller
- Costume contest with prizes (optional participation)
- Fire performers for dramatic finale
- Period dancing instruction and group dances
