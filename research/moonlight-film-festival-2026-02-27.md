# Moonlight Film Festival - Event Research
**Generated:** February 27, 2026  
**Status:** Ideating  
**Target Audience:** 150-300 guests (outdoor community experience)  
**Budget Target:** $40-55/person

## Concept Overview
An enchanting outdoor cinema experience under the stars. Guests lounge on blankets, bean bags, and vintage furniture while watching a curated film (classic romance, cult favorite, or SF-set film). The evening features a themed cocktail bar, gourmet popcorn station, food trucks, and pre-film DJ set. Think Dolores Park movie nights meets upscale outdoor lounge.

**Experience Design:**
- **Arrival (7:00-8:00 PM):** Gates open, guests claim spots, DJ playing, cocktails + food
- **7:30 PM:** Welcome toast, film introduction by local film critic/personality
- **8:00-10:30 PM:** Film screening under the stars (sunset timing perfect in spring/summer)
- **Intermission (midway):** Stretch break, refill drinks, socialize
- **Post-Film (10:30-11:00 PM):** DJ returns, mingling, photo ops with film-themed backdrop
- **Seating:** Mix of picnic blankets, bean bags, vintage chairs, low tables (layered lounge vibe)
