# Botanical Cocktail Lab - Event Research
**Generated:** February 27, 2026  
**Status:** Ideating  
**Target Audience:** 60-100 guests (interactive social experience)  
**Budget Target:** $50-65/person

## Concept Overview
An interactive botanical mixology experience where guests become cocktail scientists. Held in a lush garden/conservatory setting, attendees rotate through hands-on stations learning about herbs, botanicals, and craft cocktails. Each guest creates 3 custom drinks guided by expert mixologists, with light botanical-inspired bites and a finale cocktail competition.

**Experience Design:**
- Welcome botanical spritz on arrival (herb-infused cocktail)
- 3 rotating cocktail stations (60-min rotations, groups of 20)
  - **Station 1:** Herb Garden Bar - fresh herb cocktails, muddling techniques
  - **Station 2:** Floral Infusions - edible flowers, syrups, garnishes
  - **Station 3:** Botanical Spirits - gin botanicals, vermouth tasting, bitters
- Light botanical bites throughout (edible flower canapés, herb-infused cheeses)
- Finale: Guest cocktail competition with voting + prize
- Botanical takeaway: small herb plant or infused syrup bottle
