# Tokyo Night Market - Japanese Street Food Festival
**Research Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Concept Status:** Ideation (Research Only - No Vendor Contact)  
**Target Capacity:** 150-200 guests  
**Venn Brand Alignment:** ★★★★★ (Unique cultural immersion, social connection through shared food experiences)

## Concept Overview
Transform a historic SF warehouse into an authentic Japanese night market (yatai-style). Multiple food stations serving street food favorites (takoyaki, yakitori, ramen, taiyaki), sake/Japanese whiskey bar, traditional games (kendama, hanetsuki), paper lantern lighting, and live taiko drumming performance. Immersive experience celebrating Japanese festival culture.
