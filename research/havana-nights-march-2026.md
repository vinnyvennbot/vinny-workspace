# Havana Nights - Cuban Social Dancing & Rum Tasting
**Research Date:** March 10, 2026
**Event ID:** TBD (pending database creation)

## Concept Overview
Immersive Cuban cultural experience combining salsa/bachata social dancing with premium rum tasting, live Latin band, authentic Cuban cuisine, and vintage Havana aesthetic. Celebrates Latin culture in SF's diverse community while creating authentic social connection through partner dancing.

**Target Audience:** 25-45, culturally curious, social dancers (beginner to advanced), Latin American community, couples & singles
**Capacity:** 150-200 guests
**Duration:** 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM (5 hours)
**Ideal Timing:** Friday or Saturday evening
