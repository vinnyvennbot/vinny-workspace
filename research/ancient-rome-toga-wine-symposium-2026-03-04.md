# Ancient Rome: Toga & Wine Symposium
**Research Date:** March 4, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-rome-mar04
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO EXTERNAL CONTACT)

## Event Concept
An immersive journey to ancient Rome combining wine education, classical entertainment, Roman cuisine, and toga-clad revelry. Guests experience a traditional Roman symposium (drinking party) with educational wine tasting, live classical music, Roman games, and period-appropriate feast.

**Target Audience:** 28-45 year olds, wine enthusiasts, history buffs, educated professionals

**Capacity:** 80-120 guests
**Duration:** 4 hours (evening)
**Price Point Target:** $75-95/person

## Theme Elements
- Toga dress code (required - simple DIY instructions provided)
- Roman wine tasting with sommelier education
- Classical musicians (lyre, flute, ancient instruments)
- Roman feast with period-authentic dishes
- Laurel wreath making station
- Roman games and activities (dice, board games)
- Classical poetry/philosophy readings
- Reclining dining setup (pillows and low tables)
