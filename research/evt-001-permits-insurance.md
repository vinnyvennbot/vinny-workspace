## 3. MECHANICAL BULL LIABILITY WAIVERS

### **MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS**

**Waiver Content Must Include:**
- Express assumption of risk
- Release of liability for injuries
- Indemnification agreement
- Certification of physical/mental fitness
- Certification not under influence of drugs/alcohol
- Agreement to follow all safety rules

**Age Requirements:**
- **18+ years:** Can sign own waiver
- **Under 18:** Parent/guardian must sign on-site
- Consider age minimum (e.g., 12+) for safety

**Vendor Responsibility:**
- Mechanical bull vendor (Astrojump/Party Jump) typically provides waivers
- Confirm this during vendor negotiation
- Vendor should collect and retain all signed waivers

### **Our Event-Specific Considerations**

**Alcohol + Mechanical Bull = High Risk:**
- Waivers must explicitly state riders cannot be intoxicated
- Staff must monitor and refuse rides to visibly drunk guests
- Consider cut-off time (e.g., bull closes 30 min before bar closes)

**Waiver Collection Process:**
- Digital waivers (tablet/iPad) faster than paper
- Can collect during ticket check-in
- Store securely for 2-3 years minimum

### **Sample Waiver Language** (to review with lawyer):
```
MECHANICAL BULL RIDING RELEASE OF LIABILITY

I acknowledge that mechanical bull riding involves inherent risks including but not limited to falls, collisions, and physical injury. I certify that I am physically and mentally able to participate, am not under the influence of alcohol or drugs, and assume all risks. I hereby release [Venn Social/Venue/Vendor] from all liability for injuries sustained during this activity.

Signature: _______________ Date: _____ Age: _____ (if under 18, parent signature required)
```

### **Action Items**
