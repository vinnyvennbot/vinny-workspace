# Roaring 20s Murder Mystery Mansion
**Research Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Event Type:** Interactive Theater Experience  
**Target Capacity:** 80-120 guests  
**Target Demographic:** 25-40, mystery/theater enthusiasts, social groups

## Concept
An immersive 1920s murder mystery experience set in a historic SF mansion. Guests arrive in period attire, receive character assignments, and participate in solving a glamorous murder while enjoying speakeasy cocktails and jazz.

## Venue Options

### 1. Haas-Lilienthal House (RECOMMENDED)
- **Location:** 2007 Franklin St, Pacific Heights
- **Capacity:** 100 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $3,500-5,000 for evening rental
- **Pros:** Authentic Victorian mansion, perfect period atmosphere, historic landmark
- **Cons:** May require special permits for evening events
- **Contact:** San Francisco Architectural Heritage

### 2. The Flood Mansion (Heritage)
- **Location:** 2222 Broadway St
- **Capacity:** 120 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $5,000-7,000
- **Pros:** Grand ballroom, prestigious location, full kitchen access
- **Cons:** Higher price point
- **Contact:** Heritage SF

### 3. Presidio Officers' Club
- **Location:** 50 Moraga Ave, Presidio
- **Capacity:** 100 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $2,500-4,000
- **Pros:** Historic building, existing relationship with Presidio venues, flexible spaces
- **Cons:** Less mansion-like atmosphere
- **Contact:** Presidio Trust (we have existing relationships here)

## Vendor Categories Needed

### Entertainment
- **Murder Mystery Troupe:** Professional actors to run the experience
  - Options: The Murder Mystery Company SF, Corporate Game, local theater groups
  - Est. Cost: $1,500-2,500

### Music
- **Jazz Band:** 3-4 piece period-appropriate ensemble
  - Style: Hot jazz, swing, Charleston-era
  - Est. Cost: $800-1,500

### Catering
- **1920s-Inspired Menu:** Finger foods, passed hors d'oeuvres
  - Oysters Rockefeller, deviled eggs, canapés, champagne service
  - Est. Cost: $35-50/person

### Bar
- **Speakeasy Cocktails:** Period-appropriate drinks
  - Sidecars, French 75s, Bee's Knees, gin rickeys
  - Est. Cost: $15-25/person (consumption bar)

### Decor & Props
- **Period Decor:** Art deco accents, feather centerpieces, vintage props
- **Costume Elements:** Extra accessories for guests
- Est. Cost: $800-1,200

### Photography

- Est. Cost: $500-800
