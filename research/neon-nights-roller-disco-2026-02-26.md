# Neon Nights: Roller Disco Revival - Event Concept Research
**Generated:** February 26, 2026, 10:00 AM PST  
**Status:** Ideating (Awaiting Approval)  
**Theme:** 70s/80s high-energy roller skating party with neon vibes

## Concept Overview
A throwback roller disco party combining the nostalgia of 70s/80s skating culture with modern DJ production and neon aesthetics. Guests skate under disco balls and blacklights to funk, disco, and 80s hits. Think: retro vibes meet modern nightlife energy.

**Target Audience:** 150-300 young professionals (21-35) seeking active social experiences, nostalgic fun, unique date nights  
**Ideal Season:** Year-round (indoor venues preferred for consistency)  
**Experience Goals:** Active social bonding, nostalgic play, high-energy atmosphere, viral Instagram moments

## Vendor Categories Needed

### Entertainment
- **DJ:** Specializing in 70s/80s funk, disco, pop (must understand skating energy flow)
- **Skate Instructors:** Roaming "skate angels" to help beginners (2-3 staff)
- **Skate Dancers:** Pro skaters performing choreographed routines (optional wow factor)

### Production
- **Lighting:** Disco balls, blacklights, neon strips, moving spotlights, haze machines
- **Décor:** Neon signs ("Groove Zone," "Skate or Die"), 80s posters, inflatable palm trees
- **AV:** Professional sound system (critical for skating energy)

### Rentals
- **Skates:** If venue doesn't provide - need 300+ pairs in all sizes
- **Safety Gear:** Wristguards, knee pads available (optional but smart)
- **Photo Booth:** Retro 80s backdrop with props (leg warmers, headbands, sunglasses)

### Catering
- **Style:** Food trucks outside (if space allows) OR finger foods/pizza inside
- **Bar:** Retro cocktails (Blue Lagoon, Tequila Sunrise, Sex on the Beach)
- **Dietary:** Easy grab-and-go options (skating = active, need simple food)

### Photography/Videography
- **Roaming photographer:** Action shots of skaters mid-move
- **Drone video:** If outdoor venue (rooftop shots)
- **GoPro stations:** Mounted cameras for skater POV clips

### Logistics
- **Insurance:** CRITICAL - skating = liability risk (event insurance + waiver system)
- **EMT/First Aid:** On-site medical professional (non-negotiable for safety)
- **Security:** Crowd control, alcohol management if bar present

## Unique Selling Points
- **Active social experience:** Unlike standing-around parties, skating forces interaction
- **Nostalgia factor:** Taps into powerful 70s/80s cultural memories
- **Instagram gold:** Neon + movement + retro aesthetic = viral content
- **Skill leveler:** Everyone looks silly skating = breaks down social walls
- **Date night appeal:** Fun, active, nostalgic = perfect couple activity

---

## Risk Mitigation
- **Liability:** Mandatory waivers, on-site EMT, event insurance with skating coverage
- **Skill gaps:** Skate instructors roaming, beginner-friendly "slow skate" periods
- **Alcohol + skating:** Strict wristband system, cut-off times, security monitoring
- **Weather (outdoor venues):** Backup indoor plan or rain date policy
