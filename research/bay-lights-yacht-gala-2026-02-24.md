## Concept Overview
Sail into San Francisco's golden hour aboard a luxury yacht, cruising past Alcatraz, under the Golden Gate Bridge, and along the glittering city skyline. This floating gala combines breathtaking bay views, gourmet dining, live entertainment, and the romance of being on the water as the city lights up at dusk.

**Experience Pillars:**
- **Cinematic Journey:** Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, Bay Bridge, SF skyline
- **Luxury Setting:** Multi-level yacht, panoramic decks, elegant interior salons
- **Culinary Excellence:** Plated dinner or premium passed hors d'oeuvres, open bar
- **Celebration Atmosphere:** Dancing, live music, champagne toasts at iconic moments
