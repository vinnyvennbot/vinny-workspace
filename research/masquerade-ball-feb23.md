# Masquerade Ball - Venetian Mystery Night
**Date:** February 23, 2026  
**Researcher:** Vinny  
**Target Capacity:** 200-250 guests  
**Target Budget:** $75-95/person (premium experience)

## Event Concept
Elegant masquerade ball in a historic SF ballroom. Venetian masks required, live string quartet, champagne towers, mystery entertainment. Old-world European elegance meets San Francisco sophistication.

**Unique Selling Points:**
- Costume-required (masks provided or BYOM)
- Historic venue with chandeliers and ornate details
- Live classical/jazz hybrid music
- Mystery elements (secret performances, masked surprises)
- Premium dress code creates exclusive atmosphere

## Venue Options (6 Researched)

### 1. Julia Morgan Ballroom (Downtown SF)
- **Style:** Timeless, elegant, old-world charm
- **Features:** Grand architectural details, sophisticated atmosphere
- **Location:** Heart of downtown San Francisco
- **Est. Capacity:** 200-250
- **Est. Cost:** $8,000-12,000
- **Pros:** PERFECT for masquerade theme, iconic SF venue
- **Cons:** Premium pricing

### 2. The Regency Ballroom
- **Capacity:** 1,400 max (can section off for 200-250)
- **Features:** Neoclassical, 35-foot ceilings, 22 teardrop chandeliers, blonde hardwood
- **Architecture:** Turn-of-the-century, horseshoe-shaped balcony
- **Website:** theregencyballroom.com
- **Est. Cost:** $6,000-9,000
- **Pros:** Spectacular architecture, large capacity flexibility
- **Cons:** May feel too large if not full

### 3. Fairmont Hotel - Venetian Room / Gold Ballroom
- **History:** First hotel after 1906 earthquake, centennial restoration
- **Features:** Gold-leaf ceilings, crystal chandeliers, Venetian styling
- **Style:** Light, airy, elaborately detailed
- **Est. Capacity:** 200-300
- **Est. Cost:** $10,000-15,000
- **Pros:** Venetian theme PERFECT match, world-class landmark
- **Cons:** Highest cost option

### 4. Filoli Historic House (Woodside - South of SF)
- **Style:** Georgian revival mansion, 16 acres formal gardens
- **Features:** Estate ballroom seats 150, multiple outdoor ceremony locations
- **Location:** Woodside (30 min south of SF)
- **Capacity:** 150 (ballroom)
- **Est. Cost:** $7,000-10,000
- **Pros:** Authentic historic mansion, garden access
- **Cons:** Smaller capacity, outside SF proper

### 5. Great American Music Hall
- **History:** Rich history, grand architecture
- **Style:** Elegant Bay Area landmark
- **Location:** Heart of San Francisco
- **Est. Capacity:** 200-300
- **Est. Cost:** $5,000-8,000
- **Pros:** Historic venue, central location
- **Cons:** More music-hall than ballroom feel

### 6. Omni San Francisco - Grand Ballroom
- **Features:** Crystal chandeliers, elegant carpet, timeless luxury
- **Style:** Sophisticated, adaptable seating
- **Location:** Downtown SF hotel
- **Est. Capacity:** 200-300
- **Est. Cost:** $8,000-11,000
- **Pros:** Luxury hotel amenities, professional event staff
- **Cons:** Hotel ballroom (less historic character)
