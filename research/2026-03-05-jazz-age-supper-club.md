# Jazz Age Supper Club - Event Concept Research
**Date Generated:** March 5, 2026
**Concept:** 1920s-inspired dinner party with live jazz in Art Deco ballroom
**Target Audience:** Sophisticated adults (30-55), history enthusiasts, jazz lovers, vintage culture fans
**Capacity Target:** 80-150 guests
**Budget Target:** $75-95/person (premium event)

## Concept Overview
Step into the Roaring Twenties for an elegant evening of live jazz, gourmet dinner, craft cocktails, and period glamour. Guests dress in 1920s attire (encouraged), enjoy a seated multi-course dinner in an Art Deco ballroom, dance to live jazz bands, and experience the elegance of a bygone era. Think Great Gatsby meets SF sophistication - intimate supper club, not massive party.

## Key Insights
- **Premium Positioning Required:** This is NOT a budget event - lean into luxury
- **Ticket Price:** $85-125/person is justified for multi-course dinner + live jazz + historic venue
- **Sponsorship Still Needed:** Even at premium pricing, need $15,000-24,000 sponsor
- **Target Audience:** Wealthier demographic (30-55) who value experiences over price
- **Dress Code = Marketing:** "1920s attire encouraged" creates Instagram moments + excitement
- **Smaller = Better:** 75-120 guests feels exclusive vs 200+ which dilutes supper club intimacy

## Differentiation Strategy
1. **Authentic Period Venue:** Bently/Julia Morgan actually from 1920s (not themed replica)
2. **Sit-Down Dinner:** Not cocktail party, full multi-course meal (premium experience)
3. **Live Jazz:** Not DJ, actual musicians playing period-appropriate music
4. **Educational Element:** Share history of 1920s SF, Prohibition stories, venue history
5. **Dress Code:** Encourage vintage attire (creates community + photo opportunities)

## Potential Series Concept
- **Monthly Supper Club:** Rotating themes (1920s, 1940s, 1960s) in historic venues
- **Build Community:** Regular attendees, members-only future events
- **Lower Venue Costs:** Negotiate series discount vs one-off rental
