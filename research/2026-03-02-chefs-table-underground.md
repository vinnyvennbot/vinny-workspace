# Chef's Table Underground
**Research Date:** March 2, 2026  
**Target Capacity:** 40-60 guests  
**Vibe:** Intimate multi-course dining experience in secret location, chef interaction, wine pairings

## Concept
An exclusive, invitation-only chef's table experience in an undisclosed location (revealed 48h before event). Guests enjoy a 5-7 course tasting menu prepared by a celebrated SF chef, with wine pairings and behind-the-scenes kitchen access. Limited to 40-60 guests for intimacy. Dress code: Smart casual to cocktail.
