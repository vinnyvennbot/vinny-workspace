# Casablanca Dreams - Moroccan Feast & Storytelling Night
**Research Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Concept Status:** Ideation (Research Only - No Vendor Contact)  
**Target Capacity:** 60-100 guests (intimate feast)  
**Venn Brand Alignment:** ★★★★★ (Immersive cultural storytelling, intimate social connection)

## Concept Overview
Transport guests to a Moroccan riad for an evening of North African hospitality. Low seating on plush cushions around communal tables, traditional 5-course Moroccan feast (tagines, couscous, bastilla), mint tea ceremony, live oud musician, professional storyteller sharing Berber folk tales, henna artist, and Moroccan wine tasting. Intimate, sensory-rich experience emphasizing conversation and cultural immersion.
