# Parisian Cabaret Rouge: Moulin Rouge-Inspired Soirée
**Research Date:** March 6, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-cabaret-mar06
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO VENDOR CONTACT)

## Theme & Experience Design
Bring 1920s Parisian cabaret glamour to SF with burlesque performances, champagne towers, French cuisine, and intimate table seating. Think Moulin Rouge meets modern sophistication.

**Target Audience:**
- Ages 28-50
- Theater/performance enthusiasts
- Upscale nightlife seekers
- Couples and groups celebrating special occasions

**Capacity Target:** 120-180 guests (intimate setting)
**Price Point:** $85-120/person (premium positioning)
