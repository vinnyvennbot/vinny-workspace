# Prohibition Detective Night - Event Research
**Generated:** March 13, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase)
**Target Audience:** 60-100 guests (intimate, interactive)
**Price Point:** $60-75/person
**Season:** Year-round (indoor)

## Concept Overview
An immersive 1920s murder mystery/detective experience set in an authentic speakeasy environment. Guests become detectives solving a Prohibition-era crime, interacting with actors, uncovering clues, and piecing together the mystery while enjoying period cocktails and music. 7-11 PM event with full character immersion, interactive storytelling, and climactic reveal.

## Experience Design
- **Arrival:** Secret password entry, character assignment
- **Act 1 (7:00-8:00 PM):** Scene setting, character introductions, initial clues
- **Act 2 (8:00-9:30 PM):** Investigation phase, guest interaction with actors, clue gathering
- **Intermission (9:30-9:45 PM):** Cocktails, jazz music, social mingling
- **Act 3 (9:45-10:30 PM):** Final revelations, guest theories, mystery solved
- **Finale (10:30-11:00 PM):** Winner announced, after-party, photo ops
- **Throughout:** Period cocktails, jazz trio, speakeasy atmosphere
