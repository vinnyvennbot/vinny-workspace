# Alpine Lodge: Après-Ski Party in the City
**Research Date:** March 6, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-alpine-mar06
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO VENDOR CONTACT)

## Theme & Experience Design
Bring cozy European ski lodge vibes to SF - think Aspen/St. Moritz après-ski culture without leaving the city. Fondue, mulled wine, faux fur throws, fireplace ambiance, live acoustic music, and winter cocktails. Perfect for SF's cooler months.

**Target Audience:**
- Ages 28-50
- Ski/snowboard enthusiasts (Tahoe regulars)
- Couples and friend groups
- Upscale casual crowd seeking cozy social experience

**Capacity Target:** 150-200 guests
**Price Point:** $70-90/person (includes fondue dinner)
