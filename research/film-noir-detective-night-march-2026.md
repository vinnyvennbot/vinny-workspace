# Film Noir Detective Night - Interactive Mystery Theater with 1940s Style
**Research Date:** March 10, 2026
**Event ID:** TBD (pending database creation)

## Concept Overview
Immersive 1940s film noir experience where guests become detectives solving an interactive murder mystery. Full costume encouraged (fedoras, trench coats, red lipstick), live jazz quartet, noir-themed cocktails, period-accurate set design, and professional actors guiding the mystery. Combines theater, social deduction, and vintage glamour.

**Target Audience:** 25-45, mystery/theater fans, costume enthusiasts, classic film lovers, social gamers, couples
**Capacity:** 120-150 guests
**Duration:** 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM (4 hours)
**Ideal Timing:** Friday or Saturday evening

- Murder Mystery Yacht exists BUT different execution (boat = logistics/views focus; this = theater/acting focus)

**Differentiation from Murder Mystery Yacht:** 
- Yacht = venue novelty (on the water), logistical complexity, outdoor/views
- This = theater quality, costume immersion, period authenticity, indoor control
Different value propositions, can coexist in portfolio.

**Risk:** Theater company quality is CRITICAL (bad actors = bad experience). Must vet carefully. High production complexity = more coordination needed. Sponsor dependency for profitability OR requires premium pricing ($85+).

**Potential Partnership Angle:** SF theater companies may partner for exposure (reduce actor costs in exchange for ticket allocation for their audience, cross-promotion). This could improve financial viability significantly.
