# Underground Game Club - Speakeasy Arcade & Poker Tournament
**Research Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Concept Status:** Ideation (Research Only - No Vendor Contact)  
**Target Capacity:** 100-150 guests  
**Venn Brand Alignment:** ★★★★★ (Competitive social play, 1920s aesthetic, prohibition nostalgia)

## Concept Overview
Transform a venue into a 1920s speakeasy game parlor. Guests enter through a "secret" entrance (password at the door), discover a world of vintage arcade games, billiards tables, poker tournament (Texas Hold'em with prizes), craft cocktails served in teacups, whiskey tasting station, and live jazz trio. Dress code: 1920s inspired (flapper dresses, suspenders, fedoras). Competitive yet social - games bring strangers together.
