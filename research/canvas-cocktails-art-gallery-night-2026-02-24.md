**Generated:** February 24, 2026 10:00 AM PST

**Target Audience:** Creatives, art enthusiasts, young professionals seeking culture (ages 25-40)

## Concept Overview
Transform an SF art gallery into an intimate evening of culture, cocktails, and connection. Guests explore rotating exhibitions, participate in a live art creation experience, sip craft cocktails, and mingle among contemporary works—all while local musicians provide a sophisticated soundtrack. This is the accessible art scene experience: no stuffiness, just creativity and conversation.

**Experience Pillars:**
- **Art & Culture:** Genuine gallery setting with curated exhibitions
- **Interactive Creation:** Live painting demo OR guest participation (collaborative mural, mini workshops)
- **Social Atmosphere:** Cocktails + conversation starters themed around art
- **Local Artist Support:** Platform SF artists, potential sales commission for Venn

## Key Considerations
- **Art Insurance:** Gallery may require additional insurance for valuable pieces
- **Sales Infrastructure:** If selling art, need payment processing, contracts, shipping logistics
- **Artist Selection:** Feature emerging SF artists (affordable, community-building) vs established (prestige but expensive)
- **Interactive Element:** Balance participation vs observation (not everyone wants to paint)
- **Alcohol + Art:** Need clear policies about touching artwork, spill risks
- **Capacity Management:** Gallery capacity depends on wall space + social areas
