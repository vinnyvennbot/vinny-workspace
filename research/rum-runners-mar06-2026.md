# San Francisco Prohibition: The Rum Runners Ball
**Research Date:** March 6, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-rumrunners-mar06
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO VENDOR CONTACT)

## Theme & Experience Design
1920s SF Prohibition with a Caribbean twist - tell the story of rum smuggling into SF Bay during Prohibition. Secret passwords, vintage cocktails, live jazz, and speakeasy atmosphere with tropical rum focus.

**Target Audience:**
- Ages 25-45
- History buffs and cocktail enthusiasts
- Vintage fashion lovers
- Social mixers seeking unique experience

**Capacity Target:** 180-250 guests
**Price Point:** $65-85/person
