# Sunset Disco at Sea
**Research Date:** March 2, 2026  
**Target Capacity:** 100-150 guests  
**Vibe:** Boat party with disco/funk music, sunset views, dance floor on the bay

## Concept
Dance on the water as the sun sets over the Golden Gate Bridge. A luxury yacht charter featuring disco, funk, and soul music with a live DJ, full bar, light bites, and 360° views of SF Bay. Dress code: Disco glam (sequins, platform shoes, bell bottoms encouraged).
