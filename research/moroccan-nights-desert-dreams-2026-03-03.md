# Moroccan Nights: Desert Dreams
**Research Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Event Type:** Immersive Cultural Experience  
**Target Capacity:** 100-150 guests  
**Target Demographic:** 25-45, foodies, travel enthusiasts, cultural explorers

## Concept
Transform a SF venue into a Moroccan oasis with authentic cuisine, traditional music, belly dancing, henna artists, and lounge seating on Persian rugs and floor cushions. Think: immersive souk atmosphere meets upscale dinner party.

## Venue Options

### 1. The Great Northern (RECOMMENDED)
- **Location:** 119 Utah St, Dogpatch
- **Capacity:** 150 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $3,000-4,500
- **Pros:** Large open space, easily transformable, great for immersive decor
- **Cons:** Industrial feel requires heavy decoration
- **Contact:** greatnorthernsf.com

### 2. The Palace of Fine Arts Rotunda
- **Location:** 3601 Lyon St
- **Capacity:** 120 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $5,000-7,000
- **Pros:** Architecturally stunning, already has exotic feel
- **Cons:** Expensive, may have restrictions on decor
- **Contact:** SF Recreation & Parks

### 3. SoMa StrEat Food Park (Outdoor)
- **Location:** 428 11th St
- **Capacity:** 150+ guests
- **Est. Cost:** $2,000-3,500
- **Pros:** Outdoor space perfect for tents/structure, casual vibe
- **Cons:** Weather dependent, less upscale feel
- **Contact:** somastreatfoodpark.com

## Vendor Categories Needed

### Catering
- **Moroccan Restaurant Partners:** Authentic cuisine
  - Options: Aziza, Mourad, local Moroccan caterers
  - Menu: Tagines, couscous, mezze platters, mint tea, baklava
  - Est. Cost: $40-60/person

### Entertainment
- **Live Music:** Traditional Moroccan/Middle Eastern ensemble
  - Oud, darbuka, violin
  - Est. Cost: $1,200-2,000
- **Belly Dancer:** Professional performance
  - Est. Cost: $500-800
- **Henna Artist:** Interactive element
  - Est. Cost: $400-600

### Decor & Rentals
- **Moroccan Lounge Setup:** 
  - Persian rugs, floor cushions, low tables, lanterns
  - Fabric draping, string lights, candles
  - Est. Cost: $2,500-4,000
- **Tent Structure (if outdoor):** $3,000-5,000

### Bar
- **Moroccan-Inspired Cocktails:**
  - Mint tea mojitos, pomegranate martinis, rose cocktails
  - Traditional mint tea service
  - Est. Cost: $18-25/person

### Photography
- **Atmospheric Photography:** Capture the immersive environment
- Est. Cost: $600-900
