# Event Concept Research: Moonlit Garden Soirée
**Date Created:** March 9, 2026  
**Researcher:** Vinny  
**Status:** Ideating

## Concept Overview
**Theme:** Elegant outdoor evening in a secret botanical garden setting  
**Format:** Cocktail party with botanical mixology, live jazz, garden ambiance  
**Experience:** Fairy lights, floral installations, intimate jazz performances, craft cocktails with edible flowers  
**Target Audience:** Romantic couples, design enthusiasts, nature lovers (28-45)  
**Capacity:** 100-150 guests  
**Price Point:** $60-75/person  
**Revenue Model:** Ticket sales + specialty cocktail bar, plant/lifestyle brand sponsors

- **Risk Level:** High (sponsor-dependent)

## Competitive Advantage
- **SF Lacks:** Few garden party events in premium outdoor spaces
- **Seasonality:** Spring/Summer natural demand (April-September optimal)
- **Instagram Factor:** Extremely high (garden + fairy lights + botanical cocktails)
- **Aesthetic Appeal:** Attracts design-conscious, romantic demographics
- **Repeatability:** Seasonal series potential (Spring Blooms, Summer Nights, Harvest Garden)

## Seasonality Strategy
- **Optimal Months:** April, May, June, September (weather + daylight)
- **Golden Hour Timing:** Start 6:00-6:30 PM for sunset light
- **Weather Backup:** Need venue with covered option or tent rental
