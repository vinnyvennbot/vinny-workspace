# Enchanted Forest: Fairy Tale Masquerade
**Research Date:** March 4, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-enchanted-mar04
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO EXTERNAL CONTACT)

## Event Concept
A whimsical woodland fantasy where guests enter an enchanted forest filled with fairy lights, mystical creatures, botanical cocktails, and ethereal music. Masquerade masks required, with interactive storytelling and immersive forest decor creating a magical escape from reality.

**Target Audience:** 25-40 year olds, fantasy lovers, Instagram aesthetic seekers, couples

**Capacity:** 120-180 guests
**Duration:** 4-5 hours (evening into night)
**Price Point Target:** $65-85/person

## Theme Elements
- Mandatory masquerade masks (guests bring or rent)
- Enchanted forest decor (fairy lights, moss, flowers, trees)
- Mystical character performers (fairies, forest sprites, woodland creatures)
- Botanical cocktail bar with edible flowers
- Ethereal live music (harp, strings, ambient electronic)
- Interactive fortune telling / tarot reading stations
- Secret garden photo opportunities
