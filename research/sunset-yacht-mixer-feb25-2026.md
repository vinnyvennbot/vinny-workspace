## Concept Overview
Premium networking experience aboard a private yacht during San Francisco's iconic sunset. Guests enjoy craft cocktails, light bites, and organic networking while cruising past Alcatraz, under the Golden Gate, and along the waterfront. Perfect for young professionals, creators, and community builders seeking meaningful connections in an unforgettable setting.

**Target Audience:** 50-100 professionals, creators, entrepreneurs (ages 25-40)  
**Season:** Spring/Summer (March-September optimal)  
**Duration:** 2.5-3 hours (typically 5:30-8:30 PM for sunset timing)  
**Vibe:** Sophisticated, relaxed, premium networking

## Key Insights

**Pricing Challenge:** Yacht charters are expensive relative to ticket pricing. This event works best as:
1. Premium-priced intimate experience ($120-150/ticket, 40-50 guests)
2. Sponsor-subsidized at scale (100+ guests with beverage/lifestyle sponsor)
3. Loss-leader for brand building (accept small loss for unique experience)

**Competitive Advantage:** Sunset yacht cruises are rare in SF event scene. Premium positioning as networking experience (not party boat) differentiates from tourist cruises.

**Seasonality:** Strong demand May-September. Consider multi-date series to amortize marketing costs.
