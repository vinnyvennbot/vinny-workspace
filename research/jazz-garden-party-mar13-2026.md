# Jazz Age Garden Party - Event Research
**Generated:** March 13, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase)
**Target Audience:** 80-120 guests
**Price Point:** $45-55/person
**Season:** Spring/Summer daytime event

## Concept Overview
A sophisticated 1920s-inspired garden party featuring live jazz, lawn games, champagne bar, and vintage aesthetic. Guests encouraged to dress in Jazz Age attire. Daytime event (2-6 PM) with elegant outdoor setting, combining nostalgia with social connection.

## Experience Design
- **Arrival:** Greeted with champagne cocktails, vintage photo booth
- **Activities:** Croquet, bocce ball, lawn games, live jazz performance
- **Food:** Garden-style passed appetizers, champagne bar, light bites
- **Aesthetic:** String lights, vintage furniture, floral arrangements, 1920s decor
- **Music:** Live jazz trio/quartet (2-3 hour set)
