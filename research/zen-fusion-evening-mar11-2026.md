# Zen Fusion Evening - Japanese Tea Ceremony Meets Modern Mixology
**Event ID:** evt-zen-fusion-mar11
**Generated:** March 11, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Target Capacity:** 50-80 guests
**Theme:** East-meets-West fusion experience combining traditional Japanese tea ceremony with modern craft cocktails

## Concept Overview
An elegant evening that bridges tradition and innovation. Guests experience authentic Japanese tea ceremony elements (matcha service, zen aesthetics, mindful rituals) fused with contemporary mixology using Japanese ingredients (sake, yuzu, shiso, matcha-infused spirits). The venue transforms into a serene sanctuary with tatami-style seating areas, paper lanterns, bamboo installations, and minimalist zen garden aesthetics.

**Key Experience Elements:**
- Opening traditional tea ceremony demonstration
- Stations for both matcha service and modern Japanese cocktails
- Japanese small plates (izakaya-style grazing)
- Calligraphy station with artist
- Ikebana (flower arrangement) display and mini-workshop
- Ambient koto or shamisen live music
- Zen meditation corner with cushions
- Photo moments in kimono rental station
