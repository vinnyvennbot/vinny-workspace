# Jazz Age Garden Party - Event Concept Research
**Generated:** February 26, 2026, 10:00 AM PST  
**Status:** Ideating (Awaiting Approval)  
**Theme:** 1920s Gatsby-inspired outdoor sophistication

## Concept Overview
An elegant outdoor garden party celebrating the Jazz Age. Guests dress in 1920s attire (flapper dresses, suspenders, fedoras) and experience live jazz, champagne towers, lawn games, and Art Deco-inspired décor. Think: Great Gatsby meets modern SF sophistication.

**Target Audience:** 100-200 young professionals (25-40) who appreciate vintage aesthetics, live music, and elegant socializing  
**Ideal Season:** Spring/Summer (April-September)  
**Experience Goals:** Nostalgic glamour, social connection through era immersion, Instagram-worthy moments
