# Neon Nights: 90s Rave Revival
**Research Date:** March 2, 2026  
**Target Capacity:** 200-400 guests  
**Vibe:** Warehouse rave with 90s electronic music, neon production, nostalgic vibes

## Concept
Transport guests back to the golden age of rave culture. A warehouse party featuring 90s house, techno, trance, and breakbeat with full neon production, glow sticks, projection mapping, and 90s fashion encouraged. Multi-room experience with chill zones, dance floors, and photo activations. Dress code: 90s rave attire (baggy pants, bucket hats, platform shoes, neon).
