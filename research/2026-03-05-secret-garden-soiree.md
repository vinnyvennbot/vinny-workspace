# Secret Garden Soirée - Event Concept Research
**Date Generated:** March 5, 2026
**Concept:** Spring botanical garden party with hidden SF garden setting
**Target Audience:** Young professionals (25-40), nature lovers, Instagram-focused attendees
**Capacity Target:** 80-120 guests
**Budget Target:** $50-65/person

## Concept Overview
An enchanted evening in San Francisco's most beautiful botanical spaces, blending natural beauty with curated social experiences. Guests discover hidden garden spaces, botanical cocktails, live acoustic music, and Instagram-worthy moments throughout lush greenery.

## Venue Options

### 1. San Francisco Botanical Garden - Celebration Garden (PRIMARY)
- **Location:** Golden Gate Park, 1199 9th Avenue
- **Capacity:** 100-150 guests (largest outdoor venue)
- **Features:** 
  - Newly renovated 2019
  - Iconic plants from throughout the botanical garden
  - Multiple garden "rooms" for different experiences
  - Mesoamerican ferns, Southeast Asian plants
- **Vibe:** Professional, established, permits handled
- **Estimated Cost:** $5,000-8,000 venue rental
- **Contact:** Via GGGP events team
- **Website:** gggp.org/venue/sf-botanical-garden

### 2. San Francisco Botanical Garden - Moon Garden
- **Capacity:** 30-50 guests (intimate option)
- **Features:**
  - Zen-like atmosphere
  - Feels "worlds away from the city"
  - Perfect for ceremonies/intimate gatherings
- **Vibe:** Peaceful, meditative, smaller scale
- **Estimated Cost:** $3,000-5,000 venue rental

## Key Insights
- **Premium Venue Cost:** Botanical Garden venues are expensive but provide built-in beauty
- **Sponsorship Critical:** Need $7,000-10,000 in sponsors to make financially viable
- **Alternative:** Scale down to Moon Garden (50 guests) and focus on exclusivity/premium pricing
- **Differentiation:** Botanical theme is unique in SF event market, strong Instagram appeal
- **Season:** Best for April-September (SF spring/summer weather)
