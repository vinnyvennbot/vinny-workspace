# Neon Underground Rave - Event Research
**Generated:** March 13, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase)
**Target Audience:** 150-250 guests (high-production)
**Price Point:** $35-45/person
**Season:** Year-round (indoor)

## Concept Overview
An immersive electronic music experience in a warehouse setting with world-class production - LED installations, laser shows, projection mapping, and top-tier DJ lineup. Modern underground rave aesthetic with elevated production quality. 10 PM - 3 AM event targeting electronic music fans aged 21-35.

## Experience Design
- **Arrival:** ID check, LED wristbands, coat check
- **Main Space:** Warehouse transformed with lighting/projection mapping
- **Music:** 3-4 DJ sets across house/techno/bass genres
- **Visuals:** Synchronized LED walls, lasers, haze machines
- **Chill Zone:** Lounge area with ambient lighting, seating
- **Interactive:** Live VJ mixing, reactive LED installations
