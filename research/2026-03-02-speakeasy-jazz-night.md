# Underground Speakeasy Jazz Night
**Research Date:** March 2, 2026  
**Target Capacity:** 80-120 guests  
**Vibe:** Prohibition-era immersive experience with live jazz, vintage cocktails, secret passwords

## Concept
Transport guests back to the 1920s Prohibition era with an authentic speakeasy experience. Guests receive a secret password via text 24h before the event, enter through a hidden entrance, and enjoy live jazz, craft cocktails, and period-appropriate entertainment. Dress code: 1920s attire encouraged.
