# Havana Nights: Cuban Jazz & Rum Experience
**Research Date:** March 6, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-havana-mar06
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO VENDOR CONTACT)

## Theme & Experience Design
Transport guests to 1950s Havana with live Cuban jazz, rum cocktails, salsa dancing, and vintage tropical ambiance. Focus on cultural authenticity and social dance connection.

**Target Audience:** 
- Ages 25-45
- Jazz/Latin music enthusiasts
- Cocktail culture fans
- Social dancers (beginner-friendly)

**Capacity Target:** 150-250 guests
**Price Point:** $55-75/person
