# Pirate's Cove: Maritime Adventure Night
**Research Date:** March 4, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-pirates-mar04
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO EXTERNAL CONTACT)

## Event Concept
An immersive pirate-themed adventure combining treasure hunts, maritime storytelling, rum tastings, and period entertainment. Guests become part of a pirate crew competing for hidden treasure while enjoying nautical cuisine and sea shanties.

**Target Audience:** 25-40 year olds, adventure seekers, groups looking for interactive social experiences

**Capacity:** 100-150 guests
**Duration:** 4-5 hours (evening)
**Price Point Target:** $55-75/person

## Theme Elements
- Treasure hunt with clues throughout venue
- Pirate costume contest
- Rum & nautical cocktail tasting
- Sea shanty live performance
- Maritime storytelling
- Period-appropriate decor (treasure chests, ship rigging, nautical flags)
