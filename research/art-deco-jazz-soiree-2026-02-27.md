# Art Deco Jazz Soirée - Event Research
**Generated:** February 27, 2026  
**Status:** Ideating  
**Target Audience:** 100-150 guests (elegant social dancing experience)  
**Budget Target:** $55-70/person

## Concept Overview
Step back to the Golden Age of Jazz in a stunning Art Deco ballroom. Guests don 1920s-inspired attire (encouraged, not required) and dance to live jazz from an authentic 11-piece orchestra. The evening features prohibition-era cocktails, elegant passed hors d'oeuvres, a swing dance lesson, and a Best Dressed contest. Think Great Gatsby meets modern SF sophistication.

**Experience Design:**
- **Arrival (7:00-7:30 PM):** Welcome champagne coupe, coat check, photo backdrop
- **7:30-8:00 PM:** Swing dance lesson with professional instructors (optional)
- **8:00-10:30 PM:** Live 11-piece jazz orchestra, dancing, cocktails, passed appetizers
- **9:00 PM:** Best Dressed 1920s Attire contest (winner gets prize)
- **10:30-11:00 PM:** Finale: Orchestra plays iconic Gatsby-era songs
- **Dress Code:** 1920s-inspired encouraged (flapper dresses, suspenders, fedoras) but not required
