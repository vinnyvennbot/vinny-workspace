# Bohemian Night Market - Event Research
**Generated:** March 13, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase)
**Target Audience:** 120-180 guests
**Price Point:** $40-50/person
**Season:** Spring/Summer/Fall (outdoor evening)

## Concept Overview
An eclectic evening market experience blending artisan vendors, live music, international food stalls, and bohemian atmosphere. Guests wander through curated vendor booths, enjoy live acoustic/world music, sample foods from different cultures, and shop from local makers. 6-10 PM event with sunset timing, string lights, and relaxed social vibe.

## Experience Design
- **Arrival:** Entry through decorated archway, welcome drink
- **Market Stalls:** 15-20 artisan vendors (jewelry, art, clothing, crafts)
- **Food Row:** 5-7 food vendors (tacos, dumplings, Mediterranean, desserts)
- **Music Stage:** Rotating acoustic/world music acts (3-4 performances)
- **Bar Area:** Craft cocktails, wine, local beer
- **Lounge Zones:** Moroccan rugs, floor cushions, low tables
- **Aesthetic:** String lights, lanterns, tapestries, bohemian decor

## Vendor Categories Needed

### Artisan Vendors (15-20 booths)
- **Recruited:** Local makers, artists, craftspeople
- **Categories:** Jewelry, art prints, ceramics, textiles, candles, plants
- **Model:** Vendor pays booth fee ($150-300) OR curated invites
- **Revenue:** $2,250-6,000 from vendor fees (offsets costs)

### Food Vendors (5-7 stalls)
- **Types:** International cuisine variety
- **Examples:** Taco truck, dumpling stand, Mediterranean, Thai, desserts
- **Model:** Vendor keeps sales, pays flat fee ($200-400) OR % of sales
- **Budget:** Minimal cost (vendors bring own setup)

### Music & Entertainment
**Live Music Stage**
- 3-4 acoustic/world music acts (45-60 min sets each)
- Budget: $1,500-2,500 total

**Roaming Performers**
- Fire dancers, hula hoop performers, stilt walkers (optional)
- Budget: $500-800

### Production & Decor

- String lights, lanterns, tapestries
- Moroccan rugs, floor cushions, low tables

**Sound/AV**

- Budget: $800-1,200

**Bar Setup**
- Craft cocktail bar, wine/beer
- Bar structure, glassware
- Budget: $1,000-1,500 (setup + staffing)

**Photography**
