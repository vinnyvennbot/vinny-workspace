# Neon Underground - Immersive Techno Experience in Historic Vault
**Research Date:** March 10, 2026
**Event ID:** TBD (pending database creation)

## Concept Overview
Cutting-edge underground techno experience in SF's historic bank vaults or industrial spaces. Multi-room immersive environment with world-class DJs, interactive light installations, VR experiences, and curated electronic music journey. Celebrates SF's tech culture through experiential nightlife.

**Target Audience:** 21-35, electronic music fans, tech workers, art/design community, nightlife enthusiasts
**Capacity:** 200-300 guests
**Duration:** 9:00 PM - 2:00 AM (5 hours)
**Ideal Timing:** Saturday night

## Venue Research

### Option 1: The Midway SF (PREFERRED)
- **Location:** 900 Marin St, SF (Dogpatch)
- **Capacity:** 400 indoor + 500 outdoor
- **Aesthetic:** Industrial warehouse with outdoor beer garden
- **Features:** Multiple rooms, excellent sound system, bar infrastructure, raw industrial vibe
- **Estimated Cost:** $4,000-6,000 venue rental
- **Contact:** events@themidwaysf.com
- **Why:** Perfect industrial aesthetic for underground techno, multi-room setup for music journey, established nightlife venue
- **Concerns:** Neighborhood noise restrictions (ends at 2 AM)

### Option 2: The Starline Social Club (STRONG BACKUP)
- **Location:** 2236 Martin Luther King Jr Way, Oakland
- **Capacity:** 300
- **Aesthetic:** Warehouse venue with strong sound system
- **Features:** Professional-grade Funktion-One sound, multi-level space, full bar
- **Estimated Cost:** $3,000-5,000 rental
- **Contact:** info@starlinesocialclub.com
- **Why:** Known for electronic music events, Oakland underground scene credibility
- **Concerns:** Oakland location (transit from SF), but also an advantage (later end time, lower costs)

### Option 3: Public Works SF (BOUTIQUE OPTION)
- **Location:** 161 Erie St, SF (Mission)
- **Capacity:** 300
- **Aesthetic:** Purpose-built nightclub with world-class sound
- **Features:** Custom sound system, multiple rooms, lounge areas, known for electronic music
- **Estimated Cost:** $5,000-7,000 (premium venue)
- **Contact:** bookings@publicworkssf.com
- **Why:** Premier electronic music venue in SF, built-in credibility with techno community

## Strategic Notes
- SF has huge electronic music community (underserved by generic club nights)
- Tech worker audience = high disposable income, seeks quality experiences
- Multi-room journey creates "choose your own adventure" social dynamic
- VR/interactive elements align with SF's innovation identity
- High production value justifies premium ticket pricing
- Later end time (2 AM) differentiates from typical Venn events (most end by midnight)

**Differentiation from Silent Disco:** Silent disco is novelty/gimmick. This is authentic electronic music experience with professional production. Different markets.

**Risk:** High production costs require either premium ticket pricing ($65-75) OR strong tech sponsor ($8K+). Without either, event loses money. Production complexity = more coordination needed.
