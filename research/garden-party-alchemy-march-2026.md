# Garden Party Alchemy - Botanical Cocktails & Live Floristry
**Research Date:** March 10, 2026
**Event ID:** TBD (pending database creation)

## Concept Overview
Upscale daytime garden party combining botanical mixology, live floral arrangement workshops, farm-to-table bites, and acoustic music in SF's most beautiful garden venues. Celebrates nature, craftsmanship, and sensory experiences. Instagram-worthy aesthetic meets genuine skill-building.

**Target Audience:** 25-40, predominantly female (70-80%), design/creative professionals, wellness-conscious, socially aspirational
**Capacity:** 80-120 guests (intimate scale)
**Duration:** 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM (4 hours, daytime)
**Ideal Timing:** Saturday or Sunday afternoon, April-October
