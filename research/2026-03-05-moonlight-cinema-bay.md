# Moonlight Cinema on the Bay - Event Concept Research
**Date Generated:** March 5, 2026
**Concept:** Outdoor movie screening on SF waterfront with Bay views
**Target Audience:** Couples (date night), film lovers, tourists, 25-50 age range
**Capacity Target:** 100-200 guests
**Budget Target:** $40-55/person

## Concept Overview
Classic cinema under the stars with San Francisco Bay as the backdrop. Guests enjoy iconic films projected on a large outdoor screen while seated in cozy setups (blankets, beanbags, Adirondack chairs) with views of the Bay Bridge or Golden Gate. Gourmet concessions, craft cocktails, and optional boat-based viewing for premium experience. Part movie night, part romantic SF experience.

## Vendor Requirements

### AV/Projection
- **Need:** Large outdoor movie screen, high-lumen projector, professional sound
- **For Boat:** Marine-grade equipment, screen mounted on deck or shore projection
- **For Land:** Standard outdoor cinema setup (20-30ft screen)
- **Budget:** $2,500-4,000 for professional cinema quality
- **Contacts Needed:** SF AV companies specializing in outdoor events

### Film Licensing
- **Need:** Public performance rights for chosen film
- **Cost:** $200-800 depending on film (newer = more expensive)
- **Options:** Swank Motion Pictures, Criterion licensing for classics
- **Strategy:** Choose classic films (cheaper licensing) or partner with studios

### Seating/Comfort
- **Options:**
  - Blanket picnic style (BYO or provided)
  - Beanbag chairs rental
  - Adirondack chairs
  - Premium: Director's chairs with side tables
- **Budget:** $500-1,500 depending on style
- **For Boat:** Built-in seating + deck space for blankets

## Key Insights
- **Boat Option = Premium Pricing:** Unique experience justifies higher ticket cost but requires larger sponsor
- **Land Option = More Accessible:** Lower costs, broader audience, easier to fill
- **Weather Risk:** SF fog/wind is real - need backup plan or refund policy
- **Seasonal:** Best May-September, even then bring blankets
- **Competition:** Free outdoor movies exist (The Yard) - need premium differentiation
- **Alcohol = Key Revenue:** Bar sales can offset costs significantly

## Differentiation Strategy
1. **Boat Cinema:** ONLY boat-based movie experience in SF (vs all land-based competitors)
2. **Curated Film Selection:** Not mainstream blockbusters, cult classics + SF-themed films
3. **Premium Concessions:** Gourmet snacks vs basic movie theater fare
4. **Romantic Date Night Focus:** Market as couples experience, not family event
5. **Sunset Timing:** Start with Bay sunset, movie begins at twilight (magic hour)
