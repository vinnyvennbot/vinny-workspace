# Cyberpunk Underground: Future Noir
**Research Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Event Type:** Immersive Futuristic Experience  
**Target Capacity:** 150-250 guests  
**Target Demographic:** 21-35, tech scene, electronic music fans, sci-fi enthusiasts

## Concept
Transform a warehouse or underground space into a neon-drenched cyberpunk dystopia with electronic music, VR experiences, LED art installations, futuristic cocktails, and interactive tech demos. Blade Runner meets warehouse rave meets art gallery.
