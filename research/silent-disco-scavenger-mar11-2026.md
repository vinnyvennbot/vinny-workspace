# Silent Disco Scavenger Hunt - Interactive City Exploration
**Event ID:** evt-silent-disco-scavenger-mar11
**Generated:** March 11, 2026, 10:00 AM PST
**Target Capacity:** 80-120 guests
**Theme:** Mobile party adventure combining silent disco headphones with citywide scavenger hunt

## Concept Overview
San Francisco becomes your playground in this high-energy mobile experience. Guests receive wireless headphones with multiple DJ channels and embark on a scavenger hunt through iconic SF locations. Teams complete challenges, unlock clues, dance their way through the city, and converge at a final rooftop venue for the after-party. This combines fitness, exploration, social connection, music, and gamification into one unforgettable evening.

**Key Experience Elements:**
- Opening rally at central SF location (Union Square, Embarcadero, Dolores Park)
- Wireless silent disco headphones with 3 channels (90s hits, current EDM, hip-hop)
- Teams of 4-6 compete in scavenger hunt across SF neighborhoods
- Challenges: photo ops at landmarks, dance-offs in public spaces, trivia questions, local business partnerships
- Mobile app for clue delivery, point tracking, live leaderboard
- 2-3 hour hunt through 8-12 locations across SF
- Final convergence at rooftop venue for after-party with DJ, food trucks, prizes
- Prizes for winning team, best photos, most creative challenge completion
- Costume theme: "San Francisco Tourist Chic" (optional)
