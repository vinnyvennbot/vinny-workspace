# Event Concept Research: Time Traveler's Table
**Date Created:** March 9, 2026  
**Researcher:** Vinny  
**Status:** Ideating

## Concept Overview
**Theme:** Underground supper club journey through decades (1920s → 2020s)  
**Format:** Multi-course dinner where each course represents a different era  
**Experience:** Period-appropriate music, cocktails, storytelling, and decor transitions  
**Target Audience:** Young professionals, foodies, culture enthusiasts (25-40)  
**Capacity:** 60-80 guests  
**Price Point:** $85-110/person  
**Revenue Model:** Ticket sales + bar, sponsors (spirits brands for historical cocktails)

## Budget Model (70 guests @ $95/ticket)

### Revenue
- Ticket Sales: 70 × $95 = **$6,650**
- Bar Revenue: 70 × $15 avg = **$1,050**
- Sponsorships: **$5,000** (2 sponsors @ $2,500 each)
- **Total Revenue: $12,700**

### Expenses
- Venue Rental: **$3,500**
- Catering (5-course): 70 × $55 = **$3,850**
- Bar/Cocktails: **$800** (ingredients, glassware)
- DJ/Music: **$1,000**
- Photography: **$800**
- Decor/Production: **$2,000**
- Marketing: **$500**
- Platform Fees (Luma 5%): **$333**
- Insurance/Permits: **$300**
- Contingency (5%): **$600**
- **Total Expenses: $13,683**

### Financial Analysis
- **Net Margin:** -$983 (requires sponsor optimization or ticket price increase)
- **Break-Even:** $98/ticket OR 1 additional sponsor ($2,500)
- **With $5K sponsors:** Small profit margin
- **Risk Level:** Medium (sponsor-dependent)

## Competitive Advantage
- **Unique Format:** No other SF events do decade-progression dinners
- **Storytelling:** Not just food/music, but immersive narrative experience
- **Shareability:** High Instagram/social media potential (costume encouraged)
- **Repeatability:** Can become a series (different decade themes each time)
