# Silent Disco Rooftop Party - Event Research
**Date:** February 23, 2026  
**Researcher:** Vinny  
**Target Capacity:** 200-250 guests  
**Target Budget:** $55-70/person

## Event Concept
Multi-channel silent disco on a San Francisco rooftop with skyline views. Guests wear wireless LED headphones and switch between 3 different DJ channels/genres in real-time.

**Unique Selling Points:**
- Noise ordinance friendly (silent to outside)
- 3 simultaneous music experiences
- LED headphones glow different colors per channel
- Stunning SF skyline backdrop
- Late-night capability without neighbor complaints

## Budget Breakdown (200 guests @ $60/person = $12,000 revenue)

### Option A: City View at Metreon
- Venue rental: $5,000
- Silent disco equipment (Where's My Disco): $4,000
- 3 DJs: $4,500
- Marketing/photography: $1,000

- **Profit Margin:** -$2,500 (break-even at $72.50/ticket)

### Option B: Shelby's Rooftop (Lower Cost)
- Venue rental: $4,000
- Silent disco equipment: $3,500
- 3 DJs: $4,500
- Marketing: $800
- **Total Cost:** $12,800
- **Profit Margin:** -$800 (break-even at $64/ticket)

### Option C: Zen Compound (Budget-Friendly, 100 capacity)
- Venue rental: $3,000
- Silent disco equipment (100 headsets): $2,000
- 3 DJs: $4,500
- Marketing: $500
- **Total Cost:** $10,000
- **Revenue (100 @ $60):** $6,000
- **Profit Margin:** -$4,000 (break-even at $100/ticket)

**Recommendation:** Option B (Shelby's Rooftop) - Best balance of capacity, cost, and rooftop experience. Near break-even at target price.
