# 80s/90s Nostalgia Night - Event Research
**Date:** February 22, 2026  
**Researcher:** Vinny  
**Target Capacity:** 150-250 guests  
**Target Budget:** $50-75/person

## Event Concept
Immersive 80s/90s throwback party featuring:
- Classic arcade games (Pac-Man, Street Fighter, Mortal Kombat, pinball)
- Retro music (old-school hip hop, pop, new wave, grunge)
- Neon aesthetic and 90s pop art décor
- Themed cocktails and craft beer
- Optional costume contest (80s/90s attire encouraged)

**Market Validation:** Recent Luma registrations for "Party Like 2016 - Nostalgia Night" indicate strong demand for retro-themed events.

## Venue Research (11 Options)

### Tier 1: Perfect Theme Fit

#### 1. Retro Junkie (Walnut Creek)
- **Address:** 2112 N Main St, Walnut Creek
- **Size:** 8,000 sq ft immersive 80s/90s space
- **Features:** 90s grunge and neon pop art décor, retro arcade games, pinball, live DJs
- **Capacity:** 200+ (estimated from size)
- **Contact:** https://retrojunkiebar.com/
- **Pros:** PERFECT theme alignment, largest dedicated retro space
- **Cons:** Walnut Creek location (not SF proper)
- **Estimated Cost:** $3,000-5,000 venue rental

#### 2. Emporium San Francisco
- **Address:** 616 Divisadero St, San Francisco (Historic Harding Theater)
- **Phone:** (504) 208-4987
- **Features:** Largest selection of arcade/pinball, pool, air hockey, shuffleboard, full stage, live DJs
- **Capacity:** Groups up to 50+ (free tables), larger buyouts available
- **Group Pricing:** 
  - Unlimited game tokens: $20/person (full party participation required)
  - Open bar wristbands: Available for groups 50+
- **Contact:** https://www.emporiumarcadebar.com/locations/san-francisco/
- **Pros:** Iconic SF location, 100-year-old venue, full bar + games
- **Cons:** May require venue buyout for 150+ guests
- **Estimated Cost:** $5,000-8,000 venue buyout + $20/person tokens

#### 3. Thriller Social Club (SoMa)
- **Address:** 508 4th St, San Francisco
- **Features:** $85K golf simulator, 8-lane Skee-Ball, endless arcade games (Pac-Man, Mortal Kombat, Street Fighter)
- **Size:** Two-floor space
- **Capacity:** 200+ (estimated)
- **Contact:** https://www.thrillersocialclub.com/
- **Pros:** Brand new (opened 2025), enormous space, SoMa location
- **Cons:** May be pricey due to new/premium setup
- **Estimated Cost:** $6,000-10,000 venue rental
