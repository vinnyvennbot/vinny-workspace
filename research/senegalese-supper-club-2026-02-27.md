# Senegalese Supper Club - Event Research
**Generated:** February 27, 2026  
**Status:** Ideating  
**Target Audience:** 40-60 guests (intimate cultural experience)

## Concept Overview
An intimate West African culinary journey featuring authentic Senegalese cuisine with live Afrobeat/jazz fusion music. Guests experience teranga (Senegalese hospitality) through a multi-course tasting menu, storytelling, and cultural immersion in a historic SF mansion setting.

**Experience Design:**
- Welcome with bissap (hibiscus drink) and traditional greeting
- 5-course Senegalese tasting menu with chef presentation
- Live Afrobeat/jazz fusion band (2-3 piece)
- Cultural storytelling between courses
- Interactive element: guests learn traditional Senegalese dance
- Market-style dessert station with African sweets
