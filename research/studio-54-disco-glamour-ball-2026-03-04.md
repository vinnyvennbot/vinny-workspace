# Studio 54 Tribute: Disco Glamour Ball
**Research Date:** March 4, 2026
**Event Concept ID:** EVT-studio54-mar04
**Status:** Ideating (Research Phase - NO EXTERNAL CONTACT)

## Event Concept
A tribute to the legendary Studio 54 era, celebrating 1970s disco glamour with live DJs, roller disco elements, vintage cocktails, and high-fashion costume contest. Think sequins, platform shoes, disco balls, and pure dance floor energy.

**Target Audience:** 25-45 year olds, disco lovers, fashion-forward crowd, nostalgia seekers

**Capacity:** 200-300 guests
**Duration:** 6 hours (8 PM - 2 AM)
**Price Point Target:** $60-80/person

## Theme Elements
- Studio 54-style door policy (dress code enforced)
- Disco ball spectacular with professional lighting
- 70s costume contest with prizes
- Vintage cocktail menu (Harvey Wallbanger, Tequila Sunrise, etc.)
- Professional disco DJ + live funk/soul band
- Photo booth with 70s props
- VIP "velvet rope" section
