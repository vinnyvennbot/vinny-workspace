# Victorian Secret Garden Soirée
**Research Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Event Type:** Elegant Garden Party  
**Target Capacity:** 80-120 guests  
**Target Demographic:** 28-50, romantic event seekers, garden enthusiasts, vintage lovers

## Concept
An elegant Victorian-era garden party in a lush SF garden or conservatory. Guests in period-inspired attire enjoy afternoon tea service, croquet, string quartet, botanical cocktails, and stroll through curated garden spaces. Think Bridgerton meets SF botanical beauty.

## Venue Options

### 1. Conservatory of Flowers (RECOMMENDED)
- **Location:** 100 John F Kennedy Dr, Golden Gate Park
- **Capacity:** 100 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $5,000-7,000
- **Pros:** Stunning Victorian greenhouse, perfect atmosphere, iconic SF location
- **Cons:** High cost, strict vendor requirements
- **Contact:** SF Rec & Parks Special Events

### 2. Shakespeare Garden (Golden Gate Park)
- **Location:** Martin Luther King Jr Dr near California Academy
- **Capacity:** 80-100 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $2,000-3,500
- **Pros:** Authentic garden setting, more affordable, permits easier
- **Cons:** Weather dependent, fewer amenities
- **Contact:** SF Rec & Parks

### 3. Presidio Garden Wedding Venue (Arguello Gate)
- **Location:** Arguello Blvd, Presidio
- **Capacity:** 120 guests
- **Est. Cost:** $3,000-4,500
- **Pros:** Existing relationship with Presidio venues, beautiful gardens, covered areas
- **Cons:** Less Victorian architecture
- **Contact:** Presidio Trust (existing contact: Katie)

## Vendor Categories Needed

### Catering
- **Victorian Tea Service:** Multi-tier service
  - Finger sandwiches, scones with clotted cream, petit fours
  - Tea selection, champagne service
  - Est. Cost: $45-60/person

### Entertainment
- **String Quartet:** Classical/Victorian-era music
  - 2-hour performance
  - Est. Cost: $1,200-1,800
- **Garden Games:** Croquet equipment, lawn games
  - Est. Cost: $200-400

### Decor & Rentals
- **Victorian Garden Party Aesthetic:**
  - Lace tablecloths, vintage china, parasols
  - Floral centerpieces, garden arches, bunting
  - Est. Cost: $1,500-2,500

  - Est. Cost: $800-1,200

### Bar

### Photography

### Additional
