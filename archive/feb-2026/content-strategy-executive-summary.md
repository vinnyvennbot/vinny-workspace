# Content & Marketing Strategy Executive Summary
**Venn Social Events: March 7 Western Line Dancing & February 28 Intimate Dinner**

## Strategy Overview

Created a comprehensive content marketing strategy leveraging Venn Social's unique positioning as curator of story-driven SF experiences in historic venues. Strategy combines authenticity, exclusivity, and visual storytelling to drive event attendance and brand building.

## Key Research Insights Applied

- **Visual-first content** drives premium experience marketing
- **Exclusivity messaging** increases perceived value and demand
- **Cowboy Core trend** provides organic momentum for Western event
- **Micro-influencer partnerships** deliver higher ROI than macro partnerships
- **Behind-the-scenes content** builds anticipation and authenticity
