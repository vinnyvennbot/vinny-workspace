## Partnership Proposal Template

**Subject:** "Exclusive SF Experience Partnership with Venn Social"

**Opening:**
"Hi [Influencer Name],

I'm reaching out from Venn Social, SF's curator of unique, story-driven experiences in historic venues. We've been following your content and love how you showcase [specific content they create] - it perfectly aligns with our brand's focus on authentic, premium experiences that create lasting memories."

**Event Description:**
"We'd love to invite you to experience [Event Name] on [Date] as our content partner. This [event description] represents exactly the kind of unique SF experience your audience would love to discover."

**Partnership Value:**
- Complimentary tickets for you and a guest
- Behind-the-scenes access and content opportunities
- Professional photo sharing rights
- Feature opportunity on Venn Social channels
- Future event preview access

**Content Expectations:**
- 1 preview post/story before the event
- Live documentation during the experience
- 1 detailed post within 48 hours after the event
- Authentic experience sharing aligned with your voice

**Next Steps:**
"If this sounds like a great fit, I'd love to send over more details and discuss how we can create content that provides value to both your audience and ours. When would be a good time for a brief call this week?"

## Budget Allocation Recommendations

### Per Event Influencer Budget:
- **Macro Influencers (100K+):** $500-1000 value (tickets + payment)
- **Mid-Tier (10K-100K):** $200-500 value (tickets + experiences)
- **Micro (1K-10K):** $50-200 value (primarily tickets + access)

### ROI Expectations:
- Every 10K followers should generate 50-100 ticket inquiries
- Authentic micro-influencer partnerships often outperform macro for engagement
- Long-term partnerships provide better value than one-off collaborations

## Success Metrics for Partnerships

### Quantitative Metrics:
- Reach and impressions from influencer content
- Direct website traffic from influencer links
- Ticket sales attributed to influencer codes
- Social media engagement on influencer posts
- Email signups from influencer audiences

### Qualitative Metrics:
- Content quality and brand alignment
- Audience sentiment and engagement quality
- Long-term relationship potential
- Brand credibility enhancement
- Community building and network effects

This influencer strategy creates a scalable system for ongoing partnerships while ensuring each collaboration provides authentic value to both the influencer's audience and Venn Social's brand positioning as SF's premier experience curator.
