## 📊 CONVERSION STRATEGY

### Qualification Questions (Discovery Call)
1. What's your typical team event budget?
2. How many people would attend? (120-150 is sweet spot)
3. What type of events have resonated best with your team?
4. Any themes/interests? (science, space, learning experiences)
5. Timeline? (We can execute in 6-8 weeks)

### Objection Handling
**"$50K is too expensive"**
→ Break down per-person cost ($333-417/person for full venue buyout + premium experience)
→ Compare to typical SF team events ($75-150/person for basic experiences)

**"We already have team events planned"**
→ Position as quarterly or annual flagship event (not replacing monthly happy hours)
→ Offer to schedule 3-6 months out

**"We need more flexibility on attendees"**
→ Offer scaled pricing (90 people minimum, 200 people maximum)

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: Follow-up if no response
- Day 7: Final follow-up with "Should I close your file?" angle
- Week 3: Re-engage with new angle (different event, partnership opportunity)
