## Contact Research Methodology

### LinkedIn Approach (For AI Companies: OpenAI, Anthropic, Scale AI)

**Why LinkedIn:** Personalized connection, can reference company mission/values

**Process:**
1. Search: "OpenAI People Operations" (or HR, Culture, Events)
2. Identify: Head of People, VP of Culture, Events Manager
3. Connect: Send connection request with note:
   - "Hi [Name], I work with SF tech companies on unique team experiences. Wanted to connect as I have something your team at [Company] might love. Happy to share details if you're open to it!"
4. Wait for acceptance (24-48 hours)
5. Send message: Full pitch (see email template above, adapted for LinkedIn)

**Pros:** Warmer intro, can see mutual connections, higher response rate  
**Cons:** Requires acceptance first, slower process

---

### Email Approach (For SaaS/Fintech: Stripe, Salesforce, etc.)

**Why Email:** Faster, can reach multiple contacts simultaneously

**Process:**
1. Find company email format: firstname.lastname@company.com (use Hunter.io or company website)
2. Guess email: Use common patterns
   - **Stripe:** firstname@stripe.com
   - **Salesforce:** fname.lname@salesforce.com
   - **Coinbase:** fname.lname@coinbase.com
3. Verify: Use email verification tool (NeverBounce, ZeroBounce) OR just send
4. Send: Cold email (template above)

**Pros:** Faster, can batch send  
**Cons:** Lower response rate, risk of spam filters

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections + Responses

**"That's expensive."**
- Response: "I hear you. Let me break down the value. For 250 people, that's $200/person for a private planetarium experience at one of SF's most iconic venues. Compare that to a standard venue rental + catering ($150-200/person) without the wow factor. Plus, this supports the Cal Academy's educational mission - a nice CSR tie-in."

**"We already have our team events planned for the year."**
- Response: "Totally understand. Can I keep you on the list for Q1 2027? Or, if you ever need a backup option or a special surprise event, this is perfect for those moments."

**"We need to compare this to other options."**
- Response: "Absolutely, smart to explore. Happy to provide references from other tech companies who've done planetarium events, or we can schedule a quick site visit so you can see the space firsthand. What would be most helpful?"

**"Our budget is allocated differently."**
- Response: "Got it. What's your typical per-person budget for team events? We might be able to adjust the package - for example, heavy apps instead of full dinner, or a shorter experience to hit your number."

**"We prefer doing something more interactive/team-building focused."**
- Response: "Great point. The planetarium show itself is passive viewing, but we can add interactive elements - constellation mapping team activity, astronomy trivia competition, or a post-show discussion led by a Cal Academy scientist. Would that be more aligned with what you're looking for?"

**"How do we know people will actually enjoy it?"**
- Response: "That's the best part - astronomy is universally fascinating. We've found that even people who don't consider themselves 'science people' are blown away by the immersive dome experience. Plus, it's something people genuinely haven't done before, which makes it memorable. Happy to connect you with other companies who've done similar events for testimonials."

**Risk: Cal Academy venue not actually available for corporate buyouts**
- **Mitigation:** Call today to confirm (highest priority)
- **Backup Plan:** Pivot to Chabot Space Center (Oakland) or alt venues

**Risk: $50K price point too high for most companies**
- **Mitigation:** Offer tiered packages
  - **Premium:** $50K (250 people, full experience)

- **Mitigation:** Ask about budget cycles in discovery calls, adjust urgency framing

**Risk: Low response rates to cold outreach**
- **Mitigation:** A/B test subject lines, try LinkedIn + email dual approach, offer free site visit (no commitment)

**Risk: Site visits scheduled but no conversions**
- **Mitigation:** Improve proposal quality, add testimonials (request from Cal Academy), bring Zed to close larger deals
