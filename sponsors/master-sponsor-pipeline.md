## 📧 OUTREACH PROTOCOL

### Contact Research (2-3 hours per wave)
1. Find partnerships/marketing contact email
2. Identify decision maker (name + title)
3. Research recent brand activations (Instagram, press)
4. Note any SF Bay Area presence/events

### Email Template Structure
**Subject:** "[Brand] x Venn Social: [Event Type] Partnership"

**Body:**
- **Para 1:** Hook (unique SF experience, brand alignment)
- **Para 2:** Who we are (premium SF events, engaged audience)
- **Para 3:** Specific event fit (why this brand + this event = magic)
- **Para 4:** Package overview (investment, deliverables, ROI)
- **Para 5:** CTA (15-min call this week?)

**Tone:** Warm, professional, not salesy, storytelling-focused

### Follow-Up Cadence
- **Day 0:** Initial email
- **Day 3:** Follow-up if no response
- **Day 7:** Final follow-up
- **Day 14:** Move to "revisit later" list

### Tracking (CRM Database)
- Date contacted
- Email thread ID
- Response status (no reply, interested, call booked, declined)
- Notes on conversation
- Next action date
