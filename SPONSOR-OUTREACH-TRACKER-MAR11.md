## Follow-Up Protocol

### 24-Hour Rule
If no response within 24 hours of send:
- **Casual follow-up**: "Hey [Name], just bumping this up — would love to chat about partnership possibilities!"
- Send between 10 AM - 2 PM PST (highest email open rates)

### 48-Hour Rule (General info@ emails)
For Athletic Brewing, ClassPass, general inboxes:
- Wait 48 hours before first follow-up (slower response times)

### 1-Week Rule

- Send final follow-up: "Hey [Name], last ping on this — if timing doesn't work now, totally understand! Would love to stay in touch for future events."
