VENN SOCIAL EVENT PROPOSALS

EVENT 1: WESTERN LINE DANCING EXPERIENCE

Event Concept

"Wild West Nights" - Authentic line dancing experience combining professional instruction, live music, western food, and community atmosphere. Strong revenue potential through ticket sales ($40-70 range) with 75-100 capacity plus strategic sponsorship opportunities from western-aligned brands.

Venue Options

Venue Option 1: Presidio Log Cabin
Capacity: 75 people | Estimated cost: $28/person (venue + minimum catering)
Pros: Authentic rustic atmosphere, historic SF location, outdoor expansion potential
Cons: Limited capacity, permit requirements, weather dependency

Venue Option 2: The Chapel (Mission)
Capacity: 100 people | Estimated cost: $25/person (venue rental)
Pros: Professional sound system, large dance floor, established event venue
Cons: Less authentic western atmosphere, requires additional decor

Venue Option 3: Cowboys & Turbans (Castro)
Capacity: 75 people | Estimated cost: $24/person (venue buyout)
Pros: Existing western theme, built-in bar setup, known location
Cons: Smaller capacity, limited exclusivity factor

Recommended Venue: The Chapel
Balances profitability (100-person capacity), operational efficiency (professional systems), and revenue optimization while allowing western theming through decor.

Vendor Options

Music Entertainment:

  • Country DJ Specialist: $500 (4 hours) = $5/person at 100 capacity
  • Live Country Band (3-piece): $1,500 (3 hours) = $15/person at 100 capacity
  • Hybrid DJ + Solo Country Singer: $800 (4 hours) = $8/person at 100 capacity

Recommended: Live Country Band for authenticity and social media appeal

Line Dancing Instruction:

  • SF Dance School Instructor: $250 (1.5 hours) = $2.50/person
  • Professional Country Dance Specialist: $400 (2 hours) = $4/person
  • Competition-Level Instructor: $300 (1.5 hours) = $3/person

Recommended: Professional Country Dance Specialist for quality experience

Catering Options:

  • BBQ Specialist Full Service: $22/person (pulled pork, sides, cornbread)
  • Light Western Snacks: $10/person (wings, nachos, sliders)
  • Food Truck Partnership: $15/person (on-site authentic BBQ)

Recommended: BBQ Specialist for complete western experience

Sponsor & Partner Options

Primary Sponsors:

  • Volo Sports: $1,500 contribution + network promotion
  • Western Wear Store (Boot Barn): $800 + product prizes for competitions
  • Whiskey/Beer Brand: $1,000 + alcohol for open bar

Marketing Partners:

  • SF Social Club: Cross-promotion + member discounts
  • Country Bars in SF: Promotional partnerships, flyer distribution
  • Western Clothing Stores: In-store promotion, fashion show element

Financial Projection: $1,500 profit (16.7% margin) with 100 attendees at $65 tickets

EVENT 2: INTIMATE PRIVATE DINNER

Event Concept

"The Gathering" - Exclusive 30-person dining experience focused on connection through exceptional food, wine, and intimate atmosphere. Premium positioning creates FOMO for larger audience while building core Venn community. Revenue through $50-65 tickets plus strategic partnerships.

Venue Options

Venue Option 1: State Bird Provisions Private Room
Capacity: 30 people | Estimated cost: $102/person (minimum spend + service)
Pros: Michelin-starred reputation, incredible food, prestigious location
Cons: Significantly over $70/person budget, limited customization

Venue Option 2: Upscale Wine Bar Private Space
Capacity: 30 people | Estimated cost: $72/person (minimum spend + service)
Pros: Wine-focused atmosphere, built-in beverage program, intimate setting
Cons: Slightly over budget, limited food customization options

Venue Option 3: Historic Private Space + External Catering
Capacity: 30 people | Estimated cost: $62/person (venue + quality catering)
Pros: Under budget, fully customizable, unique Venn-branded experience

Recommended Venue: Historic Private Space + External Catering
Provides budget compliance, complete customization control, and better profit margins while delivering premium experience through curated programming.
