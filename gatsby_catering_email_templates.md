## QUICK REFERENCE: KEY MESSAGING POINTS
- **Event Size**: Always mention 1000-person scale upfront
- **Theme**: Emphasize authentic 1920s/prohibition era

- **Experience**: Ask about large-scale themed event experience
- **NO BUDGET**: Never mention budget constraints
- **Timeline**: "Moving forward with planning" (creates urgency)
- **Partnership**: Position as collaboration, not vendor relationship
