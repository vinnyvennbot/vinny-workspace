# Email Failures & Communication Lessons

## Critical Lesson: Email Verification (Feb 5, 2026)

**Problem**: Multiple vendor outreach emails failed due to invalid/unreachable addresses
- TJs House of Bounce: Connection timeout
- Mechanical Bull Party Rental: Connection refused

**Root Cause**: Assumed email formats without verification

**Solution Implemented**:
1. **Always verify emails** via Google Places before sending
2. **Research alternative contacts** (phone, website forms) 
3. **Test small batch** before mass outreach
4. **24-hour follow-up rule** for delivery failures

## Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Assume Email Formats
- **Wrong**: info@[domain].com for all vendors
- **Right**: Research actual contact emails via Google/website

### ❌ Don't Use AI Formatting in Business Emails  
- **Wrong**: Asterisks (*), bullet sections, structured formatting
- **Right**: Natural paragraph flow, human-like structure

### ❌ Don't Skip Follow-ups
- **Wrong**: Single outreach attempt
- **Right**: 24-hour follow-up rule, 3-strike maximum

### ❌ Don't Batch-Send Without Testing
- **Wrong**: Send to 20 vendors simultaneously 
- **Right**: Test 2-3 emails first, verify delivery
