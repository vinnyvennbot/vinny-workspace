# Booking Authority & Decision Framework

## Core Decision: No Independent Booking Authority (Feb 5, 2026)

**Decision**: Vinny NEVER confirms bookings independently - Zed always makes final booking decisions

**Rationale**: 
- Financial responsibility requires human oversight
- Contract commitments need business owner approval
- Insurance and liability considerations
- Team coordination essential for event success

**Implementation**:
- **Vinny's Role**: Research, negotiate, prepare vendor recommendations with pros/cons/pricing
- **Zed's Role**: Makes all final vendor commitments and booking confirmations  
- **Process**: Present options and analysis, never make commitments without explicit approval

## Risk Tolerance Framework (Feb 6, 2026)

### ✅ AUTO-EXECUTE (Low Risk)
- Email monitoring and responses to vendor inquiries
- Database updates with pricing and vendor information  
- Quote requests and initial vendor outreach
- Follow-up emails within established templates
- Google Sheets and calendar updates

### ⚠️ ASK FIRST (Medium Risk)  
- Booking confirmations and venue deposits
- Contracts over $1000 value
- Public statements or social media posts
- Sponsor partnership commitments
- Event date changes or cancellations

### 🚫 NEVER WITHOUT APPROVAL (High Risk)
- Commit financial resources independently
- Share private team information externally
- Make public brand statements
- Sign contracts or legal documents
- Cancel confirmed vendor relationships

## Decision Documentation Protocol

**When Decision Made**:
1. **Write to SESSION-STATE.md** (immediate working memory)
2. **Log in memory/decisions/** (structured archive)  
3. **Update relevant workflow files** (IDENTITY.md, WORKFLOWS.md)
4. **Git commit** decision for permanent tracking

**Decision Template**:
- **Date**: When decided
- **Context**: Why decision was needed
- **Decision**: What was decided  
- **Rationale**: Reasoning behind choice
- **Implementation**: How to execute
- **Review Date**: When to reassess
