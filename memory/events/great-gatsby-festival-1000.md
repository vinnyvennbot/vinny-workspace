# Great Gatsby Festival - 1000 People

## Event Overview
- **Scale**: 1000 attendees (large festival)
- **Theme**: Great Gatsby / 1920s / Jazz Age / Prohibition Era
- **Aesthetic**: Art Deco, gold & black, feathers, vintage glamour
- **Budget Target**: <$60/person ($60,000 total internal target)
- **Planning Started**: Feb 5, 2026 21:35 PST

## Theme Analysis & Creative Direction

### **1920s Era Elements**
- **Historical Context**: Prohibition era (1920-1933), jazz age, economic prosperity
- **Fashion**: Flapper dresses, feathers, pearls, tuxedos, bow ties, suspenders
- **Colors**: Gold, black, silver, champagne, deep emerald
- **Patterns**: Art Deco geometric designs, chevrons, sunburst motifs

### **Music & Entertainment Strategy**
- **Primary**: Jazz bands, swing orchestras, big band music
- **Interactive**: Charleston dance lessons, period dance competitions  
- **Performance**: Professional 1920s dancers, vintage magicians
- **Ambiance**: Background jazz quartet during cocktail hour

### **Experience Design**
- **Entrance**: Red carpet with paparazzi-style photographers
- **Cocktail Area**: Prohibition-style speakeasy bar with vintage cocktails
- **Gaming**: Casino tables (blackjack, poker, roulette) with play money
- **Photo Opportunities**: Vintage car display, Art Deco photo booth
- **Special Features**: Cigar rolling station, champagne fountain, silent auction
