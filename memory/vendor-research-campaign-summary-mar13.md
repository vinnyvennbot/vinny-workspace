## Success Factors

**What Worked:**
1. **Systematic batching:** 3-vendor batches prevented fatigue
2. **1-second API delays:** No rate limit issues
3. **Multi-source verification:** Official websites + directories
4. **Real-time documentation:** Immediate batch write-ups
5. **Category focus:** Completing full categories vs scattered research

**What Didn't Work:**
1. **Bartending category:** Contact form barrier (0% email success)
2. **Decor category:** High miscategorization required extra validation
3. **No geographic pre-filtering:** Wasted time on Atlanta/UK vendors

**Lessons Learned:**
1. **Category matters:** Technical vendors (Lighting) have higher email availability
2. **Service vs product:** Service vendors prefer contact forms
3. **Data quality critical:** 25% of vendors had issues (placeholders, wrong category, wrong location)
4. **Audit first:** Categories with miscategorization need pre-research audit
