## Best Practices

### Timing:
- **Mon-Wed**: 6-8 PM PST (after work, peak scroll time)
- **Thu-Fri**: 5-7 PM PST (weekend planning window)
- **Sat-Sun**: 11 AM - 1 PM PST (brunch scroll) or 7-9 PM (evening downtime)

### Hashtag Strategy:
- 5-8 hashtags max (Instagram algorithm favors quality over quantity)
- Mix of branded (#VennSocial), niche (#LineDancing, #TheBarrelRoom), and broad (#SFEvents, #SFNightlife)
- Create event-specific hashtag (#VennCountryNight or #VennNostalgia)

### Engagement Tactics:
- **Questions** in captions ("What's your go-to dance move?")
- **Tag challenges** ("Tag your dance partner", "Who are you bringing?")
- **Polls in Stories** (venue vote, theme preferences)
- **Countdown stickers** (T-7 days, T-48 hours)
- **Respond to every comment** within first hour (algorithm boost)

### Visual Style:
- Warm, inviting tones (not cold tech startup vibes)
- Mix of professional venue shots + candid behind-the-scenes
- Avoid overly polished stock photos—authenticity > perfection
- User-generated content from past events builds social proof
