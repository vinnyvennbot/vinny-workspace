# Sponsor Categories for Venn Events

## Strategic Sponsor Fit Analysis (2026-02-22)

### **Tier 1: Lifestyle & Hospitality**
- Local breweries/distilleries (beverage sponsors)
- SF restaurants (food sponsors, cross-promotion)
- Fashion/apparel brands (wardrobe sponsors for themed events)
- **Example**: Taste of the NFL model - culinary + corporate networking

### **Tier 2: Tech & Innovation**
- SF tech companies (brand activation at events)
- Event tech platforms (ticketing, experience enhancement)
- Local startups seeking community engagement
- **Rationale**: SF's tech ecosystem aligns with Venn's innovative event model

### **Tier 3: Media & Community**
- SF Chronicle/SFGATE (event sponsorship programs)
- Local media outlets (cross-promotion)
- Community organizations (mission alignment)
- **Benefit**: Audience amplification + credibility

### **Tier 4: Local Business Partnerships**
- SF Chamber members
- SoMa District businesses
- Small Business Expo network
- **Rationale**: Mutual benefit - we drive foot traffic, they sponsor experiences

## Outreach Strategy
1. Start with food/beverage (natural fit for social events)
2. Approach tech companies for themed events (Murder Mystery Yacht = startup crowd)
3. Media partnerships for marketing amplification
4. Local businesses for grassroots community building
