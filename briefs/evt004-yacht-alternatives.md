## Option 3: Multi-Yacht Fleet Approach (CREATIVE ALTERNATIVE)

### Concept
Instead of one 400-guest vessel, charter 3-4 smaller luxury yachts (100-150 capacity each) sailing together.

### Example Providers
1. **Empress Events** (150-guest luxury yacht)
   - 90-foot luxury yacht
   - Two floors, hardwood dance floor
   - Private chef, top-shelf bars
   - State-of-the-art sound system
   - Gourmet catering included

2. **Luxe Cruises** (various sizes 25-400 guests)
   - Fleet of yachts
   - Mentions murder mystery parties specifically
   - Corporate events, weddings, social events
   - Customizable experiences

3. **Yacht Connections International** (2-130 guests per yacht)
   - Multiple yacht options
   - Giants Enterprises partnership
   - Customizable to tastes and budget

### Execution
- Charter 3 yachts (Empress 150 + two 125-guest yachts = 400 total)
- Murder Mystery spans all three vessels:
  * Suspects/clues distributed across yachts
  * Radio communication between vessels
  * Finale gathering on main yacht (Empress)
- Boats sail in formation, visible to each other

### Pros
✅ More intimate luxury yacht experience (not commercial cruise)
✅ Unique twist: "Murder Across the Fleet"
✅ Multiple venues for clue stations/suspect encounters
✅ Empress has proven 5-star catering/service
✅ Flexibility if RSVP count drops below 400

### Cons
❌ Complex coordination (3 separate charters)
❌ Potentially more expensive (3x rental fees)
❌ Guests split across vessels (may miss interactions)
❌ Weather/logistics risk (boats must stay close)
❌ Harder to move between yachts during event

### Cost Estimate (Rough)
- Empress 150 guests: $15,000-25,000 (estimated)
- Two additional 125-guest yachts: $10,000-15,000 each
- **Total:** $35,000-55,000 for fleet (vs. $20,000-35,000 for single large vessel)
