**By Date:**
- Filename format: `TOPIC-MAR##-TIME.md` or `TOPIC-2026-03-##.md`
- Most recent = most relevant for current decisions

### Creating New Briefs

**Naming Convention:**
- Strategic analysis: `TOPIC-DESCRIPTION-MAR##.md`
- Event-specific: `evt###-topic-description.md`
- Process/framework: `topic-description-framework.md`
- Daily summaries: `SUMMARY-TYPE-MAR##-TIME.md`

**Required Sections:**
1. Date/time stamp
2. Problem statement or objective
3. Analysis/findings
4. Recommendations or next actions
5. Priority level (if actionable)
