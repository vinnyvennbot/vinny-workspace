## 🎯 KEY TAKEAWAYS

**What Works:**
- ✅ Partnership models (revenue share, co-hosting) >> standalone production for intimate events
- ✅ Corporate buyouts ($50-75K packages) for high-cost experiences (yacht, planetarium)
- ✅ Series/recurring models unlock long-term revenue (memberships, marketing efficiency)

**What Doesn't:**
- ❌ Small capacity (40-60 guests) + premium venues ($4-8K) = unsustainable losses
- ❌ Relying on $15-25K sponsors for single events (too difficult to secure consistently)
- ❌ One-off high-production events (roller disco, yacht gala) without partnership model

**Strategic Shift Needed:**
- **From:** "Rent venue, produce event, sell tickets, hope to break even"
- **To:** "Partner with venues/orgs, add experience layer, share revenue, minimize risk"
