## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: No Sponsor Responses
**Likelihood**: Medium (cold outreach to brands)  
**Mitigation**: 
- Warm intros through Zed's network where possible
- Emphasize local SF angle (not generic event request)
- Offer small "test" sponsorship first ($2-3K) to prove ROI

### Risk 2: Sponsor Budgets Too High
**Likelihood**: Medium (premium brands may want $15K+)  
**Mitigation**:
- Start with smaller local brands (SF Distilling, Best Day)
- Multi-event packages for larger sponsors (spread cost)
- Revenue-share model (% of ticket sales vs fixed fee)

### Risk 3: Long Sales Cycles (60-90 days)
**Likelihood**: High (corporate sponsorships are slow)  
**Mitigation**:
- Outreach NOW for events 2-3 months out (not next week)
- Pipeline multiple brands per event (backup options)
- Quick-close smaller sponsors first ($2-3K) while pursuing larger deals
