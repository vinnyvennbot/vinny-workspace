## Executive Summary

**Concept:** Immersive 80s/90s throwback party featuring arcade games, retro music, neon aesthetics, themed cocktails, and optional costume contest.

**Market Validation:** Recent Luma registrations for "Party Like 2016 - Nostalgia Night" indicate strong demand for retro-themed events in SF.

**Target Capacity:** 150-250 guests  
**Target Price:** $50-75/person  
**Estimated Profit:** Break-even to $1,500 profit (depending on venue choice)

---

## Top 3 Venue Recommendations

### Option 1: Retro Junkie (Walnut Creek) — RECOMMENDED
- **Perfect Theme Fit:** 8,000 sq ft immersive 80s/90s space with existing neon décor and arcade games
- **Capacity:** 200+ guests
- **Estimated Cost:** $13,500 total ($67.50/ticket break-even)
- **Pros:** Largest dedicated retro space, built-in theme, unique East Bay market
- **Cons:** Walnut Creek location (not SF proper)

### Option 2: Emporium SF (Divisadero)
- **Iconic SF Location:** Historic Harding Theater with 100-year history
- **Features:** Largest arcade/pinball selection in SF, full bar, stage for DJ
- **Estimated Cost:** $15,500 total ($77.50/ticket break-even)
- **Pros:** Strong SF brand recognition, proven group event experience
- **Cons:** Higher cost, may limit profit margin

### Option 3: Thriller Social Club (SoMa)
- **Brand New:** Opened 2025, two-floor premium space
- **Features:** $85K golf simulator, 8-lane Skee-Ball, endless arcade games
- **Estimated Cost:** $13,800 total ($69/ticket break-even)
- **Pros:** Modern/premium experience, SoMa location, Instagram-worthy
- **Cons:** Unknown for events (new venue), potentially higher rental cost

## Event Experience Design

**Arcade Games:**
- Pac-Man, Street Fighter, Mortal Kombat, pinball machines
- Unlimited play tokens included in ticket price

**Music:**
- Old-school hip hop, 90s pop, new wave, grunge
- Live DJ curating era-specific playlists

**Aesthetics:**
- Neon lights, 90s pop art décor (existing at Retro Junkie)
- Photo booth with retro props

**Food/Drink:**
- Themed cocktails (Blue Raspberry Kamikaze, Surge Float, Crystal Pepsi Mule)
- Craft beer selection
- Food trucks or casual bites

**Optional Add-Ons:**
- Costume contest (best 80s/90s outfit wins)
- Prizes for high scores on arcade games
