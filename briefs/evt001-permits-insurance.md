# EVT-001 — Permits & Insurance Reference
**Western Line Dancing Night | March 15, 2026 | Log Cabin at the Presidio**

---

## The Short Answer

**Venn does NOT need separate permits** for this event. Log Cabin is on NPS land but is managed by Wedgewood Events as the authorized NPS concessionaire — they hold all NPS use permits and ABC liquor licenses on Venn's behalf.

**What Venn IS responsible for:**

---

## 1. General Liability Insurance (Likely Required)

Wedgewood's event contract almost certainly requires the renter to carry event liability insurance:
- **Typical requirement:** $1–2M general liability coverage
- **Additional insured:** Policy must name Wedgewood Events and/or The Presidio Trust as additionally insured
- **Check contract:** Confirm exact requirement in the Log Cabin contract

**How to get it:**
- Event liability insurance can be purchased in 24–48 hours
- Providers: Markel Event Insurance, RVNA, WedSafe, The Event Helper
- Typical cost for a 300-person private event: $75–$200 one-time
- ⚠️ **Get this BEFORE the event if required — do not wait**

---

## 2. Mechanical Bull (Astro Jump — Handled)

- Astro Jump carries their own commercial liability insurance
- **Action:** Ask Astro Jump to provide a Certificate of Insurance naming Venn Social as additionally insured
- Their contract with Venn (when signed) should confirm this

## 4. Parking / Transportation
