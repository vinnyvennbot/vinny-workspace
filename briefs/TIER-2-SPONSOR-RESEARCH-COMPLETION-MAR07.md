### 2. Locality Matters
**SF-based brands (Distillery 209, Fernet, St. George):** High priority, strong alignment  
**National brands with SF presence (Best Day):** Medium-high priority  
**International brands (No Days Wasted - Vancouver):** Medium priority, locality concern

**Recommendation:** Prioritize SF/Bay Area brands over national/international

### 3. Contact Barriers
**Email contacts found (2):** Best Day, No Days Wasted — ready to activate  
**Contact forms only (1):** SF Distilling Co — requires manual submission  
**General business contacts (4):** Restaurants/services — not relevant for sponsorship

### 4. Category Confusion
**Legitimate Sponsors:** Consumer brands with marketing budgets (beverages, wellness products)  
**NOT Sponsors:** Restaurants, venues, distributors, service businesses, event companies

**Rule:** If they SELL events/services themselves, they don't sponsor OTHER people's events.
