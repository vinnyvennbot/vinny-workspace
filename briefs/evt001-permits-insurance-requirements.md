## Insurance Requirements

### 1. General Event Liability Insurance
**Required by:** Presidio Trust (all outdoor events)

**Minimum Coverage:**
- **$1,000,000 per occurrence** for bodily injury and property damage
- Must name the Presidio Trust (or City of San Francisco if different venue) as "additional insured"
- Coverage for: bodily injury, property damage, contractual liability, personal injury

**Cost Estimate:** $200-500 for one-day event policy

**Providers:**
- Eventsguardian (specialized in special events)
- Barbary Insurance Services (SF-based)
- Standard business insurance providers (State Farm, Travelers, etc.)

### 2. Host Liquor Liability Insurance
**Required if:** Alcohol served at event (Presidio allows with permit)

**Minimum Coverage:**
- Typically $1,000,000 liquor liability
- Protects against alcohol-related accidents/injuries
- Often bundled with general event liability

**Cost Estimate:** Additional $100-300 on top of general liability

**Requirements:**
- Age verification system (21+ only)
- Adequate controls to prevent over-serving
- Not open to general public (invited guests only)

### 3. Mechanical Bull-Specific Liability
**⚠️ HIGH RISK ITEM** - Requires specialized coverage

**Why it matters:**
- Mechanical bulls carry high injury risk
- Requires insurance-approved safety equipment:
  * Soft foam head and horns
  * Inflatable landing mat
  * Safety operator (provided by vendor)
- **Most bull rental vendors carry their own insurance**

**Action Item:** Verify Astro Jump ($1,299 quote) includes:
- [ ] Proof of insurance (COI - Certificate of Insurance)
- [ ] Names Venn Social as additional insured
- [ ] Minimum $2,000,000 coverage (industry standard for inflatable/mechanical attractions)
- [ ] Safety inspection protocol

**If vendor insurance insufficient:** May need separate amusement device coverage ($300-600)

## Presidio-Specific Permits

### Special Use Permit
**Required for:** 
- Outdoor events (any gathering beyond casual picnics)
- Food trucks/catering
- Alcohol service
- Amplified music (DJ)
- Mechanical attractions (bull)

**Process:**
1. Contact: outdoorevents@presidiotrust.gov or (415) 561-4200
2. Submit application with:
   - Event description
   - Expected attendance (est. 150-200 for EVT-001)
   - Layout/floor plan
   - Vendor list (caterer, DJ, bull, etc.)
   - Proof of insurance (see above)
3. Review period: 2-4 weeks (submit ASAP if March 15 event)
4. Permit fee: TBD (varies by event size/complexity)

**Rules:** [PDF - Rules for Special Use Permits](https://wp.presidio.gov/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Rules-for-Special-Use-Permit_Final_Updated-2024-Accessible.pdf)

### Food Truck/Catering Permit
**Included in Special Use Permit** but requires:
- Vendor proof of insurance
- Health permits (vendor should have)
- Additional fees may apply

### Alcohol Permit
**California ABC Requirements:**
- If venue doesn't have liquor license → temporary special event permit required
- Application: California Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC)
- Timeline: Apply 60 days before event (URGENT if March 15)
- Alternative: Venue (Log Cabin/Golf Course) may have existing license

**Presidio Additional Requirements:**
- Must meet State Liquor License requirements (Part 8 of Special Use Permit rules)
- Insurance must include liquor liability

## Music/Entertainment Permits

### DJ/Amplified Sound
**Included in Special Use Permit** but note:
- Noise restrictions in Presidio (check with venue)
- Outdoor event hours typically 10 AM - 10 PM max
- May require sound level monitoring

**No separate entertainment permit needed** for DJ (not a formal concert/ticketed show)

### Line Dancing Instructors
**No permit required** - considered part of event entertainment, not formal performance
