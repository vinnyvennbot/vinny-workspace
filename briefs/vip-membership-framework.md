# Venn VIP Membership Framework

## Competitive Insight
**SF Social Club** generates recurring revenue with VIP tier offering:
- Early access to events
- Reduced prices
- 2-3 events/week frequency

**Opportunity**: Venn can adapt this model for premium experiential events market.

---

## Venn VIP Membership Structure

### **Tier 1: Venn Insider** ($19/month or $190/year)

**Benefits:**
- 🎫 **Early Access** to all events (24-48 hours before public launch)
- 💰 **10% discount** on all ticket purchases
- 📬 **Exclusive event previews** via email (theme reveals, venue teasers)
- 🎯 **Priority waitlist** status (skip the line when events sell out)
- 📱 **Member-only community** (private Discord or Telegram group)

**Target Audience:**
- Frequent attendees (2-3 events/year)
- FOMO-driven individuals who want guaranteed access
- Budget-conscious members who attend regularly

**Revenue Projection:**
- 50 members @ $19/mo = **$950/month** = $11,400/year
- 100 members @ $19/mo = **$1,900/month** = $22,800/year

---

### **Tier 2: Venn VIP** ($49/month or $490/year)

**Benefits:**
- ✅ **All Insider benefits** (early access, 10% discount, community)
- 🎟️ **1 complimentary ticket** per quarter (up to $75 value)
- 🥂 **VIP perks at events** (priority seating, welcome drink, exclusive meet-and-greet)
- 🎨 **Input on future themes** (quarterly member survey, vote on event concepts)
- 🎁 **Exclusive VIP-only events** (1-2 per year: intimate dinners, private tours, member appreciation nights)
- 👥 **+1 guest discount** (bring a friend for 15% off their ticket)

**Target Audience:**
- Super fans who attend 4-6 events/year
- Social connectors who bring groups
- Individuals who value exclusivity and recognition

**Revenue Projection:**
- 25 members @ $49/mo = **$1,225/month** = $14,700/year
- 50 members @ $49/mo = **$2,450/month** = $29,400/year

---

### **Tier 3: Venn Founder's Circle** ($99/month or $990/year)

**Benefits:**
- ✅ **All VIP benefits**
- 🎟️ **2 complimentary tickets** per quarter (up to $150 value)
- 🍾 **Ultra-VIP treatment** (reserved seating, premium welcome package, backstage/venue tours)
- 🤝 **Direct line to Venn team** (priority customer service, private Slack/WhatsApp channel)
- 🎤 **Event co-creation** (suggest themes, vote on venues, shape event roadmap)
- 🎁 **Exclusive Founder events** (4 per year: private dinners with Venn founders, venue scouting trips, early previews)
- 🎨 **Member spotlight** (featured in Venn social media, newsletter, community recognition)
- 🎯 **Lifetime early access** (guaranteed first-to-know for all future events)

**Target Audience:**
- Brand evangelists who attend 6+ events/year
- High-net-worth individuals who value exclusivity
- Community builders who want influence and recognition

**Revenue Projection:**
- 10 members @ $99/mo = **$990/month** = $11,880/year
- 20 members @ $99/mo = **$1,980/month** = $23,760/year

## Technical Implementation

### **Platform Options**:
1. **Patreon** (easiest, 5-12% fee)
2. **Memberful** (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction + $25/mo platform fee)
3. **Custom Stripe Subscriptions** (2.9% + $0.30, full control)

**Recommendation**: Start with **Patreon** for speed, migrate to custom Stripe later for margin.

### **Member Benefits Delivery**:
- **Early access**: Luma private event links 24-48h before public
- **Discounts**: Unique promo codes per member tier
- **Community**: Private Discord server (free)
- **VIP perks**: Manual coordination (check-in list at events)
- **Complimentary tickets**: Issue via Luma comps

## Risks & Mitigation

### **Risk 1: Low Sign-Up Rate**
**Mitigation**:
- Offer first month free trial (Insider only)
- Founding member perks (lifetime discount)
- Testimonials from early members

### **Risk 2: High Churn**
**Mitigation**:
- Quarterly member surveys (are we delivering value?)
- Exclusive member events (create stickiness)
- Personal outreach to churned members (learn why)

### **Risk 3: Event Frequency Too Low** (2/month)
**Mitigation**:
- Increase event frequency to 3-4/month (1 premium + 2-3 casual mixers)
- Partner with other SF event organizers for reciprocal member benefits
- Host member-only "bonus" events (informal happy hours, venue tours)

### **Risk 4: Cannibalization** (members skip paid tickets, use comps only)
**Mitigation**:
- Cap complimentary tickets (1-2 per quarter max)
- Track member ticket purchase behavior
- Adjust comp structure if abuse detected
