#### Overview
- **Operator:** City Experiences (formerly Hornblower Cruises)
- **Capacity:** 400 guests (standing) | 300 seated
- **Type:** Three-level luxury yacht
- **Home Dock:** Pier 3, San Francisco | Berkeley Marina (alternate)

#### Specifications
- **Length:** Approx. 150 feet
- **Decks:** 3 levels (main, second, sundeck)
- **Indoor Space:** Climate-controlled with panoramic windows
- **Outdoor Space:** Multiple open-air deck areas
- **Dance Floors:** 2
- **Bars:** 3 full-service bars
- **Restrooms:** Multiple facilities (ADA-accessible)

#### Amenities
✅ State-of-the-art sound system  
✅ Professional lighting infrastructure  
✅ Multiple AV presentation capability  
✅ Hardwood dance floors  
✅ Full galley with chef-prepared catering  
✅ Premium bar service (beer, wine, cocktails)  
✅ Climate control (year-round operation)  
✅ Panoramic viewing areas (Golden Gate, Alcatraz, Bay Bridge)  

#### Murder Mystery Suitability
- **Space Configuration:** Multiple indoor rooms allow for different mystery "scenes"
- **Acoustic Isolation:** Can have actors in different areas simultaneously
- **Theatrical Lighting:** Built-in lighting can be enhanced for dramatic effect
- **Audience Flow:** 400 guests can move between decks during investigation
- **Performance Zones:** Main deck for central drama, second deck for interrogations, sundeck for reveals

#### Pricing
- **Base Charter:** $65-$100/person (venue only, 3 hours)
- **With Buffet Dinner:** $75-$110/person
- **With Plated Dinner:** $100-$150/person
- **Full Bar:** Included in per-person pricing
- **Custom Cocktails:** Available
- **Total for 400 Guests:** $56,000-$104,000 depending on menu/service level

#### Booking Details
- **Deposit:** 25-50% at booking
- **Cancellation Policy:** Typically 60-90 days for partial refund
- **Lead Time:** 4-6 months recommended (popular venue)
- **Availability:** Check calendar early - books 6-12 months out

#### Pros
✅ **Perfect capacity match** - exactly 400 guests  
✅ **Venn has used before** - "Boots on Deck" event proven success  
✅ **All-inclusive pricing** - venue, catering, bar bundled  
✅ **Professional operation** - Coast Guard certified, fully insured  
✅ **Complete amenities** - no need for external catering/bar  
✅ **Multiple boarding locations** - flexible for guests  
✅ **Year-round capability** - indoor backup for weather  
✅ **Strong corporate track record** - experienced with large events  

#### Cons
⚠️ Less "boutique luxury" feel than smaller yachts  
⚠️ Corporate aesthetic (not ultra-custom)  
⚠️ Popular venue = books far in advance  
⚠️ Less flexibility for ultra-custom experiences  

#### Overall Assessment
**Best choice for Venn's murder mystery event.** Perfect capacity, proven track record, complete package, and strong value. The three-level design is ideal for an interactive murder mystery where guests need to move between spaces.

**Score: 95/100** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

#### Pros
✅ **True luxury experience** - boutique yacht with high-end finishes

## APPENDIX: VENN'S PRIOR YACHT EVENT

### "Boots on Deck" (Summer 2025) - Lessons Learned

**Event Details:**
- 400 guests on luxury yacht
- 150-foot, three-level vessel
- Boarding at 5:15 PM, sailed 6:00-9:15 PM
- Country/EDM theme with DJ and dancing
- Complimentary champagne + 1 drink included

**What Worked:**
✅ 400-person capacity was comfortable  
✅ 3-hour cruise was perfect duration  
✅ Staggered boarding (5:15 PM start) worked well  
✅ Multi-level vessel kept energy flowing  
✅ Golden hour departure (6:00 PM) = beautiful lighting  

**Applicable to Murder Mystery:**
✅ Same capacity (400) is proven  
✅ Same boarding timeline (5:15-6:00 PM)  
✅ Same cruise duration (3 hours)  
✅ Same multi-level layout works for interactive experience  
✅ Venn community responds well to unique yacht experiences  

**Key Takeaway:** Venn can confidently execute a 400-person yacht event. The murder mystery adds theatrical layer but core logistics are proven.
