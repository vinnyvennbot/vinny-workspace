## **Outreach Strategy**

### **Communication Standards**
- **Professional tone**: Clean, readable emails without formatting symbols
- **No budget mention**: Collect quotes first, negotiate later
- **Theme emphasis**: 1000-person Great Gatsby festival, 1920s authenticity  
- **Complete information**: Venue size, guest count, aesthetic vision, setup needs

### **Response Goals**
- **Target**: 5+ detailed responses per category minimum
- **Continue**: Outreach until response threshold achieved
- **Track**: All interactions in updated RELATIONSHIPS.md
- **Document**: Quote details, availability, setup capabilities

- **24-hour rule**: Follow up if no initial response

- **Relationship building**: Focus on partnership potential, not just immediate needs
