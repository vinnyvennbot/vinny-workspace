## EVENT 1: WESTERN LINE DANCING EXPERIENCE

### Event Idea
"Wild West Nights" - An authentic line dancing experience combining instruction, music, food, and western atmosphere. **Why it's great for Venn:** Unique, interactive social experience that gets people moving and connecting. Strong potential for viral social content and repeat attendance. **Revenue potential:** $45-65 tickets with 75-100 capacity = $3,375-6,500 gross revenue. Sponsorship opportunities with western brands and social clubs.

### Venue Suggestions

**Venue Option 1: Presidio Log Cabin**
- Capacity: 75 people
- Estimated cost: $28/person (venue + minimum catering)
- Pros: Authentic rustic vibe, historic SF location, outdoor expansion
- Cons: Limited capacity, permits needed

**Venue Option 2: Cowboys & Turbans (Castro)**  
- Capacity: 75 people
- Estimated cost: $24/person (buyout fee)
- Pros: Existing western theme, built-in bar, known location
- Cons: Smaller capacity, may feel less exclusive

**Venue Option 3: The Chapel (Mission)**
- Capacity: 100 people  
- Estimated cost: $25/person (venue rental)
- Pros: Large dance floor, professional sound, event experience
- Cons: Less authentic western atmosphere

**My Recommendation: The Chapel**
*Balancing taste, branding, and profits: The Chapel offers the best profit potential with 100-person capacity while maintaining quality with professional sound systems. The larger space allows for better sponsorship activations and higher revenue. Authentic western decor can be added to maintain brand alignment.*

### Vendor Suggestions

**DJ/Music:**
1. **Country DJ Specialist** - $500 (4 hours) = $5/person at 100 capacity
2. **Live Country Band (3-piece)** - $1,500 (3 hours) = $15/person at 100 capacity  
3. **Hybrid DJ + Solo Country Singer** - $800 (4 hours) = $8/person at 100 capacity

**Line Dancing Instruction:**
1. **SF Dance School Instructor** - $250 (1.5 hours) = $2.50/person
2. **Country Dance Specialist** - $400 (2 hours) = $4/person
3. **Competition-Level Instructor** - $300 (1.5 hours) = $3/person

**Catering:**
1. **BBQ Specialist Full Service** - $22/person (pulled pork, sides, cornbread)
2. **Light Western Snacks** - $10/person (wings, nachos, sliders)
3. **Food Truck Partnership** - $15/person (authentic BBQ truck on-site)

**My Vendor Recommendations:**
- **Music:** Live Country Band for authenticity and social media content
- **Instruction:** Country Dance Specialist for quality experience  
- **Food:** BBQ Specialist for complete western experience

## EVENT 2: INTIMATE PRIVATE DINNER

### Event Idea
"The Gathering" - An exclusive 30-person dining experience focused on connection over exceptional food and wine. **Why it's great for Venn:** Premium positioning, high per-person value, creates FOMO for larger audience. Perfect for building core community of Venn VIPs. **Revenue potential:** $50-65 tickets = $1,500-1,950 + sponsorships. High-margin event with strong word-of-mouth potential.

### Venue Suggestions

**Venue Option 1: State Bird Provisions Private Room**
- Capacity: 30 people
- Estimated cost: $102/person (minimum spend + service)
- Pros: Michelin-starred reputation, incredible food, prestigious
- Cons: WAY over $70/person budget - not viable

**Venue Option 2: Wine Bar Private Space**
- Capacity: 30 people
- Estimated cost: $72/person (minimum spend + service)
- Pros: Intimate wine-focused atmosphere, built-in beverage program
- Cons: Slightly over budget, limited food customization

**Venue Option 3: Historic Private Space + Catering**
- Capacity: 30 people
- Estimated cost: $62/person (venue + quality catering)
- Pros: Under budget, fully customizable, unique atmosphere
- Cons: More coordination required, external vendors needed

**My Recommendation: Historic Private Space + Catering**
*Balancing taste, branding, and profits: This option stays under the $70/person cost limit while allowing complete customization of the experience. We can create a more unique Venn-branded environment and achieve better profit margins while delivering high-quality food through selected catering partners.*
