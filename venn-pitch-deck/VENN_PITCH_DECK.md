## DESIGN SYSTEM

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep Black (#0a0a0a)
- Accent: Amber/Gold (#d97706, #f59e0b)
- Text: White (#ffffff) on dark, Dark Gray (#1a1a1a) on light
- Success/Growth: Green (#22c55e)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold / Inter Bold
- Body: SF Pro Text / Inter Regular
- Size: 48-72px headlines, 24-32px body

**Photography Style:**
- High energy, vibrant, colorful
- Real people, real moments (not stock photos)
- Nightlife aesthetic, golden hour, neon lights
- Community focus (groups, not individuals)

**Layout:**
- Generous white space (100px margins)
- Consistent slide numbers (bottom right)
- Logo (top left, subtle)
- Full-bleed photos where possible
