# VENN SOCIAL — Executive Summary

## The Big Idea
**Venn is building the fully autonomous live experiences company** — operating memberships, venues, events, and media to create unforgettable real-world connections for a generation drowning in digital loneliness.

---

## The Problem
75% of Gen Z report feeling lonelier than ever, despite being "connected" 24/7. The solution isn't another app—it's getting people off their phones and into real experiences.

---

## The Solution
**4 interconnected businesses:**

1. **Events** (Year 1-2) — Curated, sold-out experiences (2/month)
2. **Memberships** (Year 2) — Venn Plus ($299/year recurring revenue)
3. **Venues** (Year 2-3) — Lease → Own flagship locations (margin expansion)
4. **Media** (Year 3+) — Content from events drives sponsorships + distribution

**Powered by AI:** Autonomous event ideation, vendor research, marketing, customer matching. Lower CAC, higher margins, infinite scale.
