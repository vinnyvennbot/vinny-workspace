## Investment Thesis for Venn

**Why investors will salivate:**

1. **Proven Market** - Fever raised $500M+, Partiful $27M, Lu.ma growing fast
2. **Multiple Revenue Streams** - Memberships (recurring), events (transactions), venues (margins), media (scale)
3. **Asset-Light at Start, Asset-Heavy at Scale** - Start with events, own venues later
4. **Autonomous Operations** - AI-powered event ideation, marketing, operations (lower CAC, higher margins)
5. **Category Creation** - Not "another event platform" - building the future of live experiences
6. **SF Advantage** - Dense social graph, high disposable income, cultural trendsetter
7. **Vertical Integration** - Control full stack: discovery → ticketing → venue → experience → content

**The Big Vision:** 
Venn becomes the **Live Nation + Soho House + Complex Media** of Gen Z/Millennial experiences - autonomous, AI-powered, vertically integrated.
